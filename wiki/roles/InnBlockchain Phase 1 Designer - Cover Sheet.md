## 🚫 Hard rules (memorise — these never relax)

- **Sample Audit Report PDF is the single highest-leverage Phase 1 production item** — never ship without Vignesh compliance sign-off. Gates $10M+ Sasha + Track B Tom + Carol pre-call trust.
- **Synthetic Sample Audit MUST carry front-matter disclosure** — exact wording: "Sample reference report — methodology and findings drawn from publicly available exploit research; no client engagement." Synthetic-without-disclosure = reputational + regulatory risk.
- **Never publish Sample Audit Report or Vendor Risk Pack designed by anyone other than you** (per Toolkit hard rules) — Sudharshini + Britto cannot AI-template these.
- **Brand template is the source of truth — no ad-lib visual elements.** When Sudharshini + Britto request a new template variant, you build it; they don't create ad hoc in Canva.
- **Never use AI avatar video** (Synthesia / HeyGen) for CEO-positioned content — too obvious a synthetic tell.
- **Never use crypto-cliché visuals** (Bitcoin logo · gold coins · floating blockchain rectangles · matrix-code overlays) — wrong tone for regulated FinTech buyers.
- **Never use stock photos of "fake businesspeople shaking hands"** — uniformly unprofessional in B2B FinTech.
- **Never make regulatory judgment calls yourself** — if a slide caption or infographic label touches regulatory framing, escalate to Vignesh BEFORE publish.
