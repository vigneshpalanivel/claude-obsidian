## 🚫 Hard rules (memorise — these never relax)

- **Never use MiCA framing on RWA assets** — #1 Carol disqualifier. Applies to every scheduled post + LP copy. When in doubt, escalate to Vignesh.
- **Apply the Cross-channel cannibalisation matrix on every Mon AM batch approval** — you are the enforcement owner. Most-violated: vector 5 (≥48 hr spacing between two LinkedIn posts on same article) · vector 2 (X thread ≥24 hr staggered from LinkedIn long-form on same article).
- **Never post Company Page same day as personal feed on same topic** (cannibalises reach).
- **Steady-state aggregate cap: ≤7 LinkedIn posts/wk from one profile** — above 7 = feed reads as content firehose.
- **AI-generated copy never publishes without human regime-leak check** — run the regime-leak checklist in [[InnBlockchain - Content Repurposing Design Toolkit]] § AI Hallucination Risk before scheduling.
- **No new GA4 events without schema review** — drift breaks per-format CTR tracking in WS12.
