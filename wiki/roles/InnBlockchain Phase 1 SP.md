## 🚫 Hard rules (memorise — these never relax)

- **Never pitch MiCA framing to an RWA prospect** — #1 Carol disqualifier. RWA = MiFID II/III + Prospectus + DLT Pilot + MAR + EU Listing Act.
- **Carol asks "can I speak to the founder?" → ALWAYS escalate to Vignesh** — never SP-shield a Carol founder request.
- **Never pitch in T1** — 300-char connection request is tone only. Pitching here = ignored.
- **Never send WS9.5a first warm DM without Vignesh per-DM approval** (rev 17) — Vignesh-voice template + 24-hr SLA approval before send.
- **Never exceed 20 connection requests/day** (LinkedIn throttle threshold).
- **Never advance prospect stage without documented CRM signal** (per WS12 Pipeline-stage exit criteria — "Qualified" requires regulatory or technical specifics, not vague interest).
- **Discovery call: first 20 min listen + qualify before pitching** (rev 8 risk register · scripted framework in Discovery Call Master Sequence).
- **Never DM someone who hasn't engaged with you on WS9.5b** — that's cold outreach, belongs in WS9 structured track.

## 🚨 Vignesh escalation triggers (during call OR within 1 hr after)

- Carol explicitly asks "can I speak to the founder?" → always escalate
- FT WealthTech Sam deal value indicated **> $100k** → escalate for co-pitch
- Trioangle existing client → route to Vignesh for pricing conversation · OR if client explicitly asks for founder · OR if relationship history feels off
- Regime confusion you can't resolve (e.g., prospect insists "we need MiCA for this RWA tokenization") → escalate same-day
- New DM template variant needed → Vignesh approves before send (cannot reuse approved templates with substantial changes)
