# Delegated-Work Spot-Check Protocol

> [!IMPORTANT] Universal new-hire ramp + ongoing spot-check rates
> Risk concentrates in the first 4 weeks of each new hire's work. Spot-check 25% of output in W1–W4 of their ramp; drop to 10% from W5+ if zero regime-leak incidents detected. Applies to any role added in any phase — SP, MPs, CW, Designer all follow the same ramp.

## Per-role spot-check rates

| Delegated work | W1–W4 ramp | W5+ steady-state | Trigger |
|---|---|---|---|
| **SP DM templates** | Vignesh approves every NEW template before send (T1, T2, T3, T4 wording variations per segment) | Once approved, SP uses without per-DM review · new template variants always re-approved | Per-new-template |
| **SP strategic comments** | Vignesh shadows first 20 comments before SP goes solo · then spot-checks 25% W1–W4 of SP solo period | 10% spot-check | Per comment batch |
| **MPs visual asset output** (carousels · infographics · PDFs) | Vignesh reviews 25% before publish | 10% spot-check | Key check: did AI-generated slide copy hallucinate any regulatory framing? |
| **MPs LinkedIn posts** (Vignesh-authored content posted by MPs) | Vignesh approves final scheduled batch each Mon AM before MPs publish Mon–Fri | Same Mon AM batch approval | Weekly |
| **Sales-led close paperwork** | Vignesh signs all contracts regardless of who led the sale — contract review is the final compliance gate | Same — never delegate | Per contract |

## Ramp-down rule

> Drop W1–W4 rate from 25% → W5+ rate of 10% **only if zero regime-leak incidents detected during the ramp period**. If even one regime-leak incident surfaces during ramp, hold at 25% for another 4 weeks before re-attempting ramp-down.

## Ramp-up rule (regime-leak incident in steady-state)

If a regime-leak incident surfaces during steady-state operation, ramp the relevant role's spot-check back up to 100% for 2 weeks → 25% for the next 4 weeks → 10% steady-state. Same recovery pattern Phase 1 → Phase 2+.

## Why this is a separate doc

Same ramp pattern applies whenever a new role is added in any phase. Universal pattern · no Phase-1-specific anchors (the role names — SP, MPs, CW, Designer — are stable across phases).
