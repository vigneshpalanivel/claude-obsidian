# JPMorgan Kinexys

Kinexys is JPMorgan Chase's enterprise blockchain division, formerly branded "Onyx." It is the most prominent bank-led blockchain infrastructure in production globally.

## Founding and Rebranding

- Original unit: **Onyx** (JPMorgan's blockchain division)
- Rebranded to **Kinexys** at the Singapore Fintech Festival (November 2024)
- Core products: Kinexys Digital Payments, JPM Coin (JPMD), Digital Debt Issuance

## Key Products

### JPM Coin (JPMD)
- Deposit token representing USD held at JPMorgan
- Originally internal; extended to public blockchains (November 2025)
- Being integrated natively onto **Canton Network** via Digital Asset collaboration
- Enables programmable institutional money movement

### Kinexys Digital Payments
- 24/7 cross-border institutional payment rails
- **$2 billion+ in daily transaction volume**
- Enables real-time FX settlement in USD and EUR
- Accessible to partner banks and corporate clients

### Digital Debt Issuance
- Tokenized bonds and repo agreements settled on Kinexys infrastructure

## Recent Developments (2025–2026)

- **November 2025**: JPM Coin extended to public blockchains
- **2026**: Kinexys integrating deposit token with Canton Network (Digital Asset partnership)
- **April 2026**: CEO Jamie Dimon publicly stated JPMorgan must "move faster" on tokenization as stablecoins and faster settlement systems threaten traditional fee income

## Strategic Significance

Kinexys represents the proof point that a systemically important bank can build, operate, and scale blockchain infrastructure at enterprise volume ($2B+/day). It is the benchmark other financial institutions measure themselves against when evaluating blockchain adoption.
