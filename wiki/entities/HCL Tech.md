# HCL Tech (HCLTech)

**Type**: Indian multinational IT services and consulting company  
**HQ**: Noida, India  
**Revenue (FY25)**: ~$14B

## AI Strategy (2025–2026)

**Declared goal**: Full-stack AI offering delivering "tangible business impact" from infrastructure to consulting. Shift from horizontal AI to packaged industry solutions.

## Core Platforms
- **AI Force** — Agentic AI + GenAI automation platform; covers software/data engineering, IT operations, enterprise business processes
- **AI Foundry** — manages the "data-to-dollar value chain" for AI production lifecycle; cornerstone of overall AI program

## Full-Stack Coverage
Bottom to top:
1. GPUs/CPUs and cognitive infrastructure
2. AI models and data/AI platforms
3. Applications and products
4. AI consulting for production scale

## Key Acquisitions for AI
- **Nuance** — conversational AI and clinical NLP capabilities
- **HPE Communications Technology Group** — enterprise communications AI

## Strategic Partnership
- **OpenAI** (multi-year, 2025) — one of OpenAI's first strategic services partners; OpenAI models embedded across AI Force, AI Foundry, and industry-specific accelerators

## Competitive Differentiator
TBR (Technology Business Research) analysis: HCLTech differentiates through aligned three-pillar coordination (acquisitions + alliances + industry strategy) rather than just platform announcements. Legacy IT credibility + new AI IP = differentiated credibility with enterprise buyers.

## Revenue per Employee
+$618 per employee vs FY22 (lower gain than Infosys, but improving).
