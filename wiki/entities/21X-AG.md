# 21X AG: First EU-Licensed DLT Trading and Settlement System

**21X AG** is the first entity in the EU to receive a license to operate a fully regulated blockchain-based trading and settlement system (DLT TSS) under the [[DLT-Pilot-Regime]]. Based in Germany, regulated by **BaFin**.

---

## Key Facts

| Item | Detail |
|------|--------|
| License type | DLT TSS (Trading + Settlement System) |
| Regulator | BaFin (Germany) |
| License date | December 3, 2024 |
| Opened for trading | September 8, 2025 |
| Settlement currency | USDC (atomic, on-chain) |
| Blockchain | Technology-agnostic; Stellar integration confirmed |
| CEO | Max J. Heinzle |

---

## What 21X Does

21X enables **atomic settlement** of tokenized securities: matching and settlement happen in a single blockchain transaction, eliminating the T+2 window and counterparty risk between trade execution and settlement. This is structurally impossible in traditional market infrastructure where trading venues and CSDs are legally separate.

**Asset types**: tokenized stocks, bonds, and funds (natively digital; not wrapped versions of existing securities)

**DLT TSS advantage**: 21X can skip the traditional CSD entirely — it handles both functions under one DLT Pilot license, which is the key exemption the Pilot Regime grants.

---

## Authorization Process

The 18-month licensing process involved: **21X + BaFin + Bundesbank + ESMA + ECB** — the most complex regulatory coordination yet attempted for a DLT market infrastructure in the EU. This process is the template any new DLT TSS applicant faces.

---

## Positioning

21X calls itself a "technology-agnostic market infrastructure" — meaning it does not force issuers or traders to use a specific blockchain. The Stellar integration is the first multi-chain step; additional chains are expected. This is a key differentiator vs. infrastructure locked to a single chain.

---

## InnBlockchain Context

21X is the primary reference architecture for what a startup-built DLT TSS can achieve. Any InnBlockchain client wanting to build or apply for DLT TSS status should study 21X's architecture: atomic settlement, multi-chain support, USDC settlement layer, and the 18-month BaFin process.
