# LISE: Europe's First Tokenized Stock Exchange for SMEs

**LISE** (Lightning Stock Exchange) is Europe's first fully tokenized stock exchange, specifically focused on **SME listings and tokenized IPOs**. Subsidiary of **Kriptown** (French "neo-exchange for startups and SMEs"). Licensed as a DLT TSS under the [[DLT-Pilot-Regime]] by France's AMF and ACPR.

---

| Item | Detail |
|------|--------|
| License type | DLT TSS (Trading + Settlement System) |
| Regulator | AMF + ACPR (France) |
| License date | October 13, 2025 |
| Parent | Kriptown |
| Focus | SME tokenized IPOs; secondary trading |
| First IPO | ST GROUP — April 9, 2026 |

---

## What LISE Does

LISE combines trading and settlement in a single DLT TSS — the same structural advantage as [[21X-AG]], but focused on **small and mid-sized companies** that cannot economically access traditional IPO infrastructure (investment bank fees, CSD costs, prospectus overhead).

**Key proposition**: natively digital IPO. A company lists directly on LISE as a tokenized equity, skipping the traditional CSD. Settlement is atomic and on-chain. The entire listing process is designed to be accessible to companies that wouldn't qualify for or afford a Euronext or Deutsche Börse listing.

## Why LISE Matters

1. **First tokenized IPO in EU history**: ST GROUP's April 2026 listing on LISE is the first time a company has gone public via natively tokenized equity on a regulated EU exchange. This is a landmark that validates the entire DLT Pilot Regime for capital formation.

2. **SME access**: Europe has a well-documented SME funding gap — traditional equity markets require too much overhead for companies under €50M market cap. LISE's DLT TSS model can dramatically reduce listing costs and open public equity markets to a much wider issuer base.

3. **France-first**: France's AMF licensed LISE via the DLT Pilot Regime, positioning France as the primary EU jurisdiction for tokenized SME equity. This is notable context for InnBlockchain's EU-first positioning.

## InnBlockchain Context

LISE is the **proof of concept for the SME tokenized equity market**. For InnBlockchain clients who are:
- SMEs seeking a lower-cost IPO alternative
- Asset managers building SME equity products
- Boutique exchanges wanting to replicate the LISE model in another EU jurisdiction

LISE demonstrates that DLT TSS licensing is viable for new entrants, not just Deutsche Börse subsidiaries. It also shows that the Pilot Regime can serve capital formation (primary issuance), not just secondary trading.
