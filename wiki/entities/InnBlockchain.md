# InnBlockchain

Blockchain-native development and smart contract security firm. **Sister company to [[Trioangle]]** — both are InnBlockchain group companies. InnBlockchain delivers on-chain products; Trioangle delivers marketplace and app platforms, plus the Crypto Products clone script line that is operated under the Trioangle brand.

## Positioning

> Blockchain engineering team for regulated FinTech companies and crypto-native protocols who need to ship secure, audit-ready on-chain products fast — without hiring scarce senior Solidity/Rust/ZK engineers.

Two distinct motions:
1. **Security Audit** — review existing smart contract code pre-mainnet
2. **Development / Engineering** — build protocols, contracts, integrations from scratch or extend them

ACVs run $20k–$250k+ on the custom side. Crypto Products clone scripts run $5k–$20k.

## Service Portfolio

- Smart contract development (Solidity, Rust, Move, ZK circuits)
- Smart contract audits and pre-mainnet security reviews
- DeFi protocol engineering (DEX, AMM, lending, yield)
- RWA tokenization platforms (real estate, vehicles, commodities, private credit)
- CEX/DEX matching engines, wallet custody, fiat on/off ramps
- Web3 wallet and NFT marketplace builds
- L2 / chain infrastructure work
- AI x crypto (on-chain AI agents, AI-powered DeFi, AI model marketplaces)
- Blockchain integration for regulated FinTechs (stablecoin settlement, tokenized securities, AI audit trails)

## Group Company Routing Rules

Non-blockchain briefs (marketplace, on-demand, rental, e-learning, mobility) → route to [[Trioangle]] and [[Trioangle Channel Partner Personas]].

Blockchain-specific briefs (CEX/DEX, DeFi, NFT, Web3 wallet, token launch, regulated FinTech blockchain integration) → InnBlockchain.

Sub-$20k crypto-native end-clients → route to [[InnBlockchain Crypto Products ICP]] (Trioangle clone scripts). Sub-$20k FinTech clients → **do not** route to Crypto Products; scope smaller integration or park.

Crypto-native startup (licensed or not) → [[InnBlockchain Crypto Native ICP]]. Existing regulated FinTech adding a crypto product line → [[InnBlockchain FinTech ICP]]. Company **origin** is the gate, not licensing status.

## Geography — 5-Project EU Gate

All four ICPs share a **primary: EU Continental** focus and a **5-project gate** before activating any other geography. Until 5 EU projects close, do not invest outbound in UK, UAE, US, Singapore, MENA, or SEA. MiCA (in force) is the dominant compliance framing for EU outbound.

Watch geographies (post-gate): UK (FCA — distinct post-Brexit), MENA (ADGM/VARA/DFSA), US (FinCEN/SEC), Singapore (MAS).
