# TCS (Tata Consultancy Services)

**Type**: Indian multinational IT services and consulting company  
**HQ**: Mumbai, India  
**Revenue (FY25)**: ~$29B  
**Headcount (FY26 end)**: 584,519 (down from 607,979 in FY25, -23,460)

## AI Strategy (2025–2026)

**Declared goal**: Become "the world's largest AI-led technology services company."

**AI revenue**: $1.5B annualized (Q4 FY25), growing 16.3% QoQ  
**AI projects**: 5,500+ completed; 209 AI platforms installed for clients  
**Trained employees**: 180,000+ with advanced AI skills

## Core Platforms
- **TCS AI WisdomNext** — enterprise AI platform
- **ignio™** — cognitive automation for IT operations
- **Cognix™** — AI-powered business process automation
- **TCS MasterCraft™** — GenAI + Agentic AI for legacy system modernization (2026)

## Five Strategic Pillars
1. Internal transformation
2. Service redefinition
3. Talent development
4. Customer value chain reinvention
5. Ecosystem partnership expansion

## Key Moves (2025–2026)
- **HyperVault AI Data Centre Ltd** — new subsidiary for sovereign data centers (Oct 2025)
- **TPG collaboration** — large-scale data center investment (Nov 2025)
- Partnerships: NVIDIA, Google, Microsoft, OpenAI
- Named Leader in AI and GenAI Services by Everest Group

## Competitive Position
Largest Indian IT firm by revenue. Competing on scale: most AI projects, most trained staff, most platform deployments. CEO Krithivasan frames AI as demand-expanding, not just efficiency-enabling.
