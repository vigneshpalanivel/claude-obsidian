# Infosys

**Type**: Indian multinational IT services and consulting company  
**HQ**: Bengaluru, India  
**Revenue (FY25)**: ~$19B  
**AI as % of revenue**: 5.5% of quarterly revenue (Q3 FY26)

## AI Strategy (2025–2026)

**Declared goal**: Capture $300–400B incremental AI services market by 2030 via "AI-first value framework."

**AI projects**: 4,600+ underway  
**Top clients working with on AI**: 90% of top 200  
**New service offerings**: 30+ across six value pools

## Core Platform: Infosys Topaz Fabric
Launched 2025. Composable, interoperable stack:
- Data infrastructure
- Foundation models
- AI agents
- AI flows and orchestration  
- AI apps

Delivery with: humans-in-loop agents, autonomous end-to-end agents, augmentation agents.

## Strategic Partnerships (2026)
- **OpenAI** — enterprise software development transformation
- **AWS** — Topaz + Amazon Q Developer for enterprise GenAI
- Google Cloud, Microsoft Azure

## Hiring (Layoffs)
- 700+ campus recruits terminated early 2026
- 950+ total layoffs across rounds while officially claiming "no mass layoffs"
- Revenue per employee: +$3,295 vs FY22

## Competitive Position
Second-largest Indian IT firm. Competing on platform composability (Topaz Fabric) and breadth of AI service coverage across six value pools. Forrester named them Leader in AI Technical Services, Q4 2025.
