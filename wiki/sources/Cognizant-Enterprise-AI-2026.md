# Cognizant Enterprise AI Strategy 2026

## Market Context
- AI impacting **93% of jobs** (New Work, New World 2026 research)
- "AI mastery will be table stakes for all employees" in 2026
- Organizations moving from experimentation to deep operational embedding

## Ten Key 2026 Predictions (Leadership View)
1. Risk-aware reinvestment of early AI productivity gains
2. Context engineering becomes critical (capture undocumented human work)
3. Productivity paradox — expectations outpace reality; workforces busier
4. Organizational hierarchies dissolve, dynamic teams around objectives
5. People-technology coevolution: AI + robotics + sensors → autonomous ecosystems
6. Smart regulation (EMEA): pragmatic balance of innovation + safety
7. AI literacy as employee baseline
8. Healthcare workflow optimization (clinical docs, scheduling)
9. New payer roles: AI interpreters and ethicists in enterprise
10. End-to-end autonomy: self-orchestrating, agent-coordinated systems

## Workforce Strategy
- **Skillspring** — AI-native conversational learning platform, learning embedded in workflow
- **Synapse** — train-to-hire initiative reskilling 1M+ individuals by 2026
- **Fresher hiring plan**: 24,000–25,000 in 2026 (+20% vs 2025); emphasis on "learnability over experience"
- Junior employee uplift via Anthropic, Gemini, OpenAI partnerships

## Competitive Positioning
Cognizant positions itself as essential integration partner: system integration, data pipeline security, talent readiness. Sector-specific, context-driven solutions rather than generic AI tools.

## Notable Data Point
Cognizant rates itself as Leader in Artificial Intelligence and Generative AI Services PEAK Matrix® Assessment 2025 (Everest Group).
