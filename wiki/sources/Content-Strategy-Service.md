## CTA Strategy — Persona-Gated (Critical Rules)

Wrong CTA = disqualified leads or missed sales. These rules are non-negotiable.

| Persona | Primary CTA | Tone |
|---------|------------|------|
| Scaling CTO | "Book a Technical Architecture Review" | Technical, peer-level, data-driven |
| Founder-CTO (pre-seed articles) | "Book a free 20-min fit call — no commitment, no pitch" | Accessible, empathetic |
| Founder-CTO (post-seed articles #39, #40, #43) | "Book a Paid Discovery Sprint" | Confident, milestone-focused |
| Enterprise Buyer | "Request a Formal Proposal — up to 80% lower cost than Accenture. 16-week average delivery." | Professional, ROI-focused |
| MENA Relationship Buyer | "Connect via LinkedIn or WhatsApp — no forms, no pitch decks" | Relationship-first, respectful, NEVER transactional |

### Special CTA Rules

**Budget Overlap Zone ($30k-$40k)**: Founder-CTO and Scaling CTO budgets overlap here. Default to Persona 2 (Founder-CTO) motion unless explicit post-Series A context is confirmed. Never upgrade to Discovery Sprint CTA without budget qualifier.

**Article #79 (Salvage Failed Dev Partnership)**: Target is vendor-fatigue recovery. NEVER use a discovery call CTA. Use: *"Free 45-minute architecture review — no commercial conversation, just a technical second opinion. No forms, no pitch."* Tone must be empathetic first, never transactional.

**MENA articles (Cluster 6)**: Zero exceptions. Never use forms or response-SLA language. Always relationship-first CTA via LinkedIn DM or WhatsApp.

**Cluster 4.2 non-technical articles (#45, #46, #47, #77)**: Use qualification-first fit call CTA. These attract pre-funded founders who do not meet qualifying criteria. Do not route to Discovery Sprint.

**Cluster 4.3 compliance articles (#48, #49, #50)**: Scaling CTO only. Technical Architecture Review CTA. Never Founder-CTO framing.
