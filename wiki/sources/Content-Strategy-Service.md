## CTA Strategy — Persona-Gated

Wrong CTA = disqualified leads or missed sales. Non-negotiable rules.

| Persona | Tier | Primary CTA | Tone |
|---------|------|------------|------|
| 🎯 **P5 AI-Forward Platform Operator** | Direct primary | **Two-step funnel** — Awareness articles (#82, #83, #84, #92, #111): *"Book a free 1-hour **AI Readiness Call** — engineering-language, no slide deck"*. Decision articles (#85–#91, #102–#104, #107–#112): *"Book a paid 2-week **AI Audit Sprint** ($1.5k–$4k, fee credited)"* | Engineer-to-engineer, skeptical-of-hype, domain-proof-first; A/B-test framing required for every metric claim |
| 🎯 **P1 Scaling CTO** | Direct secondary | "Book a Technical Architecture Review" (no upfront fee). If AI scope present → route to **AI Audit Sprint** first | Peer-level technical authority |
| 🎯 **P2 Non-Technical Founder-Operator** | Direct secondary | "Book a free 20-min fit call — Existing-Operator qualification" or **paid Discovery Sprint** for post-Series-C / acquisition triggers (with AI Audit Sprint as planned follow-on) | Accessible, empathetic, business-outcome focused; established-operator framing |
| 🤝 **P3 Enterprise Buyer** | Channel only | "Request a Formal Proposal" — 80% lower cost than Accenture, 16-week average delivery. Channel-partner enablement content only | Professional, ROI-focused |
| 🤝 **P4 MENA Relationship Buyer** | Channel only | "Connect via LinkedIn or WhatsApp" — no forms, no pitch decks, ever | Relationship-first, respectful; NEVER transactional |

### Pillar 7 Banned Language

Triggers Persona 5 vendor skepticism — never use: *"AI transformation"*, *"strategy session"*, *"we can train a custom LLM for you"*, *"AI roadmap discussion"*, *"demo of our AI platform"*, *"Transform your business with AI"*, *"AI strategy consultation"*.

### Special CTA Rules

- **Article #79 (Salvage Failed Dev Partnership):** vendor-fatigue recovery — NEVER discovery call CTA. Use empathetic free architecture review.
- **MENA articles (Cluster 6):** zero exceptions. Never forms or response-SLA language.
- **Cluster 4.2 non-technical articles (#45, #46, #47, #77):** qualification-first fit call CTA (filters out pre-funded founders who don't meet Existing-Operator Filter).
- **Cluster 4.3 compliance articles (#48, #49, #50):** Scaling CTO only. Technical Architecture Review CTA.
- **Cluster 7.4 loss-reason articles:** AI Audit Sprint CTA framed around honest build-vs-buy advice — differentiator vs SaaS competitor is *"we'll tell you to use the SaaS if it fits."*
- **Implementation articles (#114, #115, #116):** existing client post-AI-feature-ship — AI Audit Sprint as "AI feature health check" or "model refresh engagement" (drives second AI project in 2–4/year repeat-buy cadence).
