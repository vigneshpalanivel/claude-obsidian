# LinkedIn Outreach Strategy — InnBlockchain

Source: `.raw/InnBlockchain - LinkedIn Outreach Strategy.md`

## Summary

Full LinkedIn outbound playbook for InnBlockchain across 5 ICP tracks. Covers Sales Navigator search filters per segment, buying triggers, 5-touchpoint message sequences with templates, daily budgets, KPI targets, disqualification signals, and common mistakes. EU Continental Europe is the sole geography for all tracks until the 5-project gate. RWA is Priority 1 across both FinTech and Crypto Native.

## ICP Track Priorities

| Priority | Track | LinkedIn Weight | Geography | Primary Personas |
|----------|-------|-----------------|-----------|-----------------|
| 1 | FinTech — RWA/WealthTech | Primary | EU Continental | Strategic Sam (CEO/CPO), Technical Tom (CTO) |
| 1 | Crypto Native — RWA | Primary | EU Continental | RWA Felix (asset-backed founder) |
| 2 | FinTech — ExchangeTech, LendTech, WalletTech | High | EU Continental | Strategic Sam, Technical Tom |
| 2 | Channel Partner P2 | Primary | EU Continental | FinTech transformation principals |
| 2 | Crypto Native — DeFi/Protocol | Trigger-based only | EU Continental | Founding Felix, Security Sasha |
| 3 | Channel Partner P1/P3 | Secondary (X/Telegram first) | EU Continental | Web3/crypto advisory founders |

## 5-Touchpoint Outreach Sequence

| Touchpoint | Timing | Action |
|-----------|--------|--------|
| T1 | Day 0 | Connection request — personalised note (300 char max) |
| T2 | Day 1-2 after acceptance | Welcome message — no pitch, open question |
| T3 | Day 5-7 | Value asset — relevant content or insight |
| T4 | Day 12-14 | Soft ask — reference their context, propose short call |
| T5 | Day 21 | Final nudge — brief, low-pressure close or park 60 days |

> Most deals close at T3-T5. Do not skip steps or compress cadence.

> T1 notes are hard-capped at 300 characters. Verify length before sending. Cut the middle clause if over — not the personalisation hook or sign-off.

## Sales Navigator Filters by Segment

### FinTech RWA/WealthTech
- Title: CTO OR CEO OR CPO OR "VP Engineering" OR "Head of Product" OR "Head of Digital Transformation"
- Industry: Financial Services
- Company size: 50-500
- Geography: EU (Germany, Netherlands, France, Ireland, Nordics, Baltics)
- Keywords: "RWA" OR "RWA tokenization" OR "tokenized securities" OR WealthTech OR MiCA OR "crypto exchange" OR "DeFi lending" OR "blockchain"
- Vertical order: WealthTech/RWA first → ExchangeTech → LendTech → WalletTech

### Crypto Native RWA
- Title: Founder OR CEO OR CTO OR "Managing Director" OR "Head of Tokenization" OR "Head of Digital Assets"
- Industry: Real Estate / Commodities / Financial Services
- Geography: EU Continental
- Keywords: "RWA tokenization" OR "real estate tokenization" OR "asset tokenization" OR "MiCA" OR "tokenized assets" OR "property on-chain"

### DeFi/Protocol (trigger-activated only)
- Identify trigger first (exploit, funding, job posting, audit post) — then search
- Title: Founder OR CEO OR CTO OR "Protocol Lead" OR "Head of Engineering" OR "Head of Security" OR "Blockchain Lead"
- Company size: 5-100
- Geography: EU Continental
- Keywords: DeFi OR "smart contract" OR "protocol security" OR Solidity OR "Web3 engineering"

### Channel Partner P2
- Title: Principal OR Partner OR "Managing Director" OR Founder OR "Practice Lead"
- Industry: Management Consulting / Financial Services
- Company size: 2-50
- Geography: EU (Germany, Netherlands, France, Switzerland)
- Keywords: "blockchain advisory" OR "FinTech transformation" OR "digital transformation" OR "tokenomics" OR "MiCA advisory"

### Channel Partner P3 (signal-activated)
- Title: Founder OR Partner OR "Regulatory Advisor" OR "Compliance Consultant" OR "Legal Counsel"
- Industry: Legal Services / Management Consulting
- Company size: 1-20
- Geography: EU (Germany, Netherlands, France, Switzerland)
- Keywords: "MiCA advisory" OR "VASP licensing" OR "crypto compliance" OR "blockchain regulatory"

## Buying Triggers (Priority Order)

### FinTech RWA (Highest Priority)
1. Post/announcement about tokenizing real assets, STOs, or tokenized securities
2. Upcoming platform launch with tokenized asset backing
3. MiCA compliance deadline or licensing push for a tokenization product
4. Job posting for "Blockchain Developer", "Solidity Engineer", or "Smart Contract Engineer"
5. New funding round announced
6. LinkedIn post about "exploring Web3" or adding blockchain infrastructure

### DeFi/Protocol (Highest Urgency Signal)
1. Comparable protocol exploited in same ecosystem — reach within 48 hours
2. Upcoming mainnet launch or audit deadline
3. New funding round announced — reach within 72 hours
4. Job posting for "Solidity Engineer" or "Smart Contract Developer" — reach within 48 hours
5. Post about audit prep or "looking for an audit partner"

## Message Templates

### FinTech RWA T1 (Strategic Sam)
"Hi [First Name], noticed [Company]'s work on [tokenized securities / STO / RWA platform]. At InnBlockchain we build the smart contract infrastructure for EU WealthTech teams — the part where a flaw doesn't just affect liquidity, it affects ownership rights and licensing. Would love to connect."

### FinTech RWA T1 (Technical Tom)
"Hi [First Name], saw [Company] is building [tokenization platform / smart contract infrastructure for assets]. I run InnBlockchain — we embed with WealthTech engineering teams on MiCA-aligned smart contract security. Always good to connect with CTOs working in this space."

### Crypto Native RWA T1
"Hi [First Name], came across your work on [real estate tokenization / commodity on-chain / RWA platform] at [Company]. At InnBlockchain we build and audit smart contract infrastructure for asset tokenization — where a flaw doesn't just drain a pool, it compromises ownership rights. Would love to connect."

### DeFi Exploit Signal T1
"Hi [First Name], saw what happened to [Protocol X] — protocols with similar [lending / DEX / staking] architecture are reviewing their contracts right now. At InnBlockchain we do rapid security reviews in 2 weeks. Worth connecting."

### DeFi Funding Signal T1
"Hi [First Name], congrats on the [Protocol]'s raise — exciting milestone. I run InnBlockchain, we work with DeFi teams who need embedded smart contract security as they scale post-raise. Always good to connect with technical founders at this stage."

### Channel Partner P2 T1
"Hi [First Name], been following your work on [blockchain transformation / MiCA advisory / tokenization strategy]. At InnBlockchain, we work as a silent delivery arm for advisory firms — handle the build phase for their clients, they keep the relationship. Thought it worth connecting."

### Universal T5 (Day 21, no response)
"Hi [First Name], just circling back from my last message — happy to leave it here if the timing isn't right. If [Company]'s blockchain build or security priorities shift in the next few months, I'm easy to reach. Good luck with [specific thing from their LinkedIn — launch / audit / product]."

## Daily Budget

- **Total**: 15-20 connection requests/day (personalised — never bulk)
- **Split**: 8-10 RWA (FinTech + Crypto Native) + 3-5 DeFi/Protocol (trigger-activated) + 2-4 non-RWA FinTech or Channel Partner
- Profile views: 80-100/day
- Messages: 30-50/day
- InMails (Sales Nav): 10-20/day

## Weekly Action Plan

| Day | RWA (Priority 1) | DeFi/Protocol (Trigger) | FinTech Non-RWA + Channel Partner |
|-----|-----------------|------------------------|----------------------------------|
| Monday | Identify 8-10 EU RWA leads; check tokenization announcements; DQ filter | Monitor Rekt News, DeFi Llama, funding announcements; identify 3-5 triggered prospects | Identify 3-5 ExchangeTech/LendTech + 2-3 Channel Partner P2 |
| Tuesday | Send 8-10 personalised RWA connection requests (asset-specific hook) | Send 3-5 trigger-based requests (each unique hook) | Send 2-4 non-RWA/Channel Partner requests — cap: 15-20/day |
| Wednesday | T2 welcome to RWA connections from Mon-Tue; engage in EU PropTech/WealthTech groups | T2 welcome to DeFi connections (security-first open question) | T2 to non-RWA/Channel Partner; T3/T4 follow-ups |
| Thursday | Publish RWA/tokenization content; T3 value asset to prior-week RWA connections | T3 value asset (security vulnerability breakdown); monitor new exploit signals | Engage in FinTech Founders groups; T3 to non-RWA |
| Friday | T4 soft-ask to qualified RWA leads (10-14 days, positive signal); book calls | T4 soft-ask (pre-audit architecture review offer); book calls | Review reply rates; T5 nudge or park stale leads; update pipeline |

## Disqualification Signals

| ICP | Disqualify If |
|-----|--------------|
| FinTech RWA/WealthTech | Idea-stage, no product, no licensing intent, no timeline |
| Crypto Native RWA | No underlying asset, no codebase, zero build budget |
| Crypto Native RWA | Jurisdiction with no regulatory clarity on tokenization |
| DeFi/Protocol | Whitepaper-only, no testnet, no build budget |
| DeFi/Protocol | No trigger identified — do not connect cold |
| FinTech (all) | "Just exploring" — no timeline, no funding, no MiCA/licensing pressure |
| Channel Partner P2 | Big4 / large consultancy |
| Channel Partner P2 | Advisory firm with its own in-house blockchain dev team |
| Channel Partner P3 | Pure law firm (legal-only, no advisory clients in build phase) |
| All | Prospect hiring multiple blockchain dev firms for competitive pitches |

## Common Mistakes (10)

1. Generic cold sequences for DeFi/protocol founders — every T1 must anchor to a specific trigger
2. Pitching in T1 — the connection note sets tone only; pitch starts at T4 earliest
3. Generic connection requests without specific reference
4. Treating FinTech RWA and Crypto Native RWA as the same audience (compliance-first vs. ownership-risk-first)
5. Mixing FinTech and Crypto Native messaging in a single sequence
6. Outreaching outside EU Continental Europe before 5-project gate — each Watch geography needs its own regulatory brief (UK: FCA, UAE: ADGM/VARA/DFSA, Singapore: MAS, US: FinCEN/SEC)
7. Exceeding 15-20 connection requests/day — account restrictions trigger at high volume
8. Stopping after T2 — most deals close at T3-T5
9. Pitching Trioangle or generic software dev — every message is InnBlockchain blockchain engineering and Web3 security only
10. Direct outreach from a LinkedIn group joined the same day — establish 2-week presence first

## Profile Prerequisite

Profile must be updated before running outreach. Minimum required:
- Headline: "CEO @ InnBlockchain | Blockchain Engineering for Regulated FinTech | RWA Tokenization · WealthTech · ExchangeTech | Web3 Security | MiCA Compliance"
- About section rewritten with ICP pain-point language
- Banner updated (remove "Turn Your Business Ideas Into Reality" / "200+ Mentored")
- Featured section: InnBlockchain service pages + 1 MiCA/RWA article
- ICP-aligned skills: Smart Contract Security, Solidity, MiCA Compliance, RWA Tokenization, DeFi

## Open Questions

- P3 follow-up cadence (T2/T3/T4) should be folded into Wednesday-Friday slots alongside other active sequences — not treated as fully ad-hoc post-connection.
- X/Telegram outreach strategy for DeFi/Protocol crypto-native segments is referenced as a separate document — not yet ingested.
