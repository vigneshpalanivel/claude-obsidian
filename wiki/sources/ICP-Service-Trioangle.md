# ICP — Trioangle Custom Development & AI Engineering

**Source**: `.raw/ICP - Service.md` | Trioangle internal | 2026 | Status: historically grounded, 2026 formal validation pending

---

## What This Document Is

Trioangle's Ideal Customer Profile for **custom development** (greenfield, platform rebuilds) and **AI engineering** (AI features added to existing platforms). Distinct from [[ICP-Product-Trioangle]] (clone scripts, $500-$25k) and [[ICP-Channel-Partner-Trioangle]] (agency intermediaries).

**Routing rule**: If buyer needs a proven-model platform launched fast with standard features, route to [[ICP-Product-Trioangle]]. If they need proprietary architecture, unique business logic, or a rebuild, they belong here.

---

## Core ICP Profile

| Attribute | Details |
|---|---|
| **Who** | Funded Startups (Seed/Series A) and Established Enterprises |
| **Size** | 3-150+ employees |
| **Revenue** | $1M+ ARR or freshly funded ($200k-$5M raised) |
| **Stage** | Seed+, Series A, Series B, or profitable businesses investing in digital infrastructure |
| **Geography** | North America, Western Europe, Australia, UAE/Saudi Arabia (MENA) |
| **Budget** | $10k (MVP/Seed) to $120k+ (enterprise digital transformation) |
| **Verticals** | 9: Delivery & Logistics, Ride-Hailing & Transport, Logistics & Fleet, Travel & Hospitality, Commerce & Marketplaces, On-Demand Home Services, Social & Entertainment, Education & E-Learning, Multi-Service / Super Apps |

**AI Engineering expansion (2026):** Domain-specific AI implementation within the same 9 verticals. Not generic AI consulting. Intelligent dispatch, dynamic pricing, demand forecasting, conversational booking, fraud detection - built by engineers who already know the domain edge cases.

Full detail: [[Trioangle Service ICP]]

| 2 | Founder-CTO (non-technical) | CEO/Co-Founder, Seed stage | $10k-$40k | 3-6 weeks |
| 3 | Digital Transformation Enterprise Buyer | Head of Digital/VP Product/CIO | $50k-$120k | 3-6 months |
| 4 | MENA Relationship Buyer | CEO/MD, UAE/KSA | $30k-$80k | 1-3 months relationship + 1-2 weeks to close |

**Qualification shortcut:** <$10k = disqualify. $10k-$40k = Persona 2 motion (speed, trust, simplicity). $40k+ = assess technical vs. relationship-led and apply corresponding persona playbook.

## Access Asymmetry: Who Can't Be Reached Directly

**Persona 3 (Enterprise Buyer)** and **Persona 4 (MENA Relationship Buyer)** are NOT reachable via Trioangle's standard direct outbound. They require channel partner intermediaries (see [[ICP-Channel-Partner-Trioangle]]):

- Enterprise Buyer: Uses formal RFP processes. Accessible only via management consulting firms already trusted by the enterprise.
- MENA Relationship Buyer: Relationship-first, referral-only. UAE/KSA-based agency or consulting partners provide warm introduction. Cold outreach does not work here.

## Situational Triggers (Buying Urgency)

Highest-value signals to watch for:
- Funding announcement (Seed, Series A, bridge) in last 90 days
- Platform outage mentioned publicly
- Job posting for CTO, VP Engineering, or Senior Backend Engineer
- Key engineering lead departure (LinkedIn)
- Company expanding to new geography or vertical
- AI strategy mentioned at board/investor level (Persona 5 trigger)

Pause signals (nurture, don't push): mid-fundraise, post-failed agency (lead with empathy, free architecture review), internal engineering hiring spree, post-acquisition integration.

## Competitive Positioning

**Primary differentiator**: 800+ domain-specific platform builds since 2015 across all 9 verticals. Domain edge cases (dispatch logic, commission flows, multi-tenant auth, real-time tracking, rental availability engines) are solved patterns, not learned on the client's budget.

**Against large consultancies (Accenture, Infosys)**: Up to 80% lower cost; 16-week average delivery vs. 18-24 months.
**Against generic AI agencies**: Domain-specific AI implementation vs. strategy decks. We know the vertical's edge cases before the first call.
**Against AI coding tools (Copilot/Cursor)**: We deliver AI features (outcomes), not AI tools (accelerators). Engineers using Copilot still need to know what to build.
