# Accenture AI Strategy 2026

## Financial Performance
- **Advanced AI revenues (Q1 FY2026)**: $1.1B (120% YoY growth)
- **Advanced AI bookings (Q1 FY2026)**: $2.2B (76% YoY growth)
- **GenAI + Agentic AI revenues (12mo ending Aug 2025)**: $2.7B (tripled YoY)
- **GenAI bookings (12mo)**: $5.9B (nearly doubled YoY)
- **Total new bookings**: $20.9B; AI represents 10.5%
- **Addressable AI market**: $70B by 2029 (40%+ CAGR)

## Key Bets
1. **Faculty acquisition** (Jan 2026, ~$1B) — 400+ AI specialists, including data scientists and engineers; Marc Warner becomes CTO
2. **AI Refinery** — enterprise AI ecosystem connector
3. **GenWizard** — IT delivery automation platform
4. **3,000+ reusable AI agents** deployed across clients

## Workforce Scale
- **80,000+ AI and data professionals** (doubled in two years)
- **550,000+ employees** trained in GenAI fundamentals ("reinventors")
- **1,300+ AI clients** served

## Strategic Framework: "Reinvention"
CEO Julie Sweet: AI is "embedded in some way across nearly everything we do." Discontinued separate AI metrics reporting — AI is no longer a standalone business unit, it's foundational.

Framing: "Cognitive Digital Brain" — elevating AI from tactical projects to fundamental business transformation.

## Key Partnerships
OpenAI, Anthropic, Snowflake, Palantir — combining industry models with Accenture delivery scale.

## Analyst Recognition
Named Leader in Gartner's inaugural Magic Quadrant for Digital Technology and Business Consulting Services (January 2026).
