## Key Facts Extracted

- **Single source of truth.** Consolidates: CITE audit (2026-06-26), execution playbook, referral/opportunity landscape (v2.1), outcomes report, and the domain/page implementation guide.
- **Target 90+ (Excellent)** from 62 baseline; framed as a 12–18 month program. 90+ is the **18-month** milestone, not 12 (Citation is the rate-limiter).
- **Guiding principle:** earn the score, don't game it — reject any tactic that lifts the number without real authority.
- **Per-dimension conversion roadmap:** all 40 items mapped to what flips each to Pass, with an **AI Citation Impact** column (High/Med/Low/None) — a separate axis from the score. Cheap points (copyright T08, cookie T04, NAP I03, social I05) are rated None for citations.
- **Opportunity Landscape:** named platforms (DA scores) mapped to the specific CITE gap each closes — product/business listings (G2 92, Capterra 93, Crunchbase 91, Trustpilot 93), editorial/press (freeCodeCamp, Entrepreneur India, Hacker Noon, YourStory, Inc42…), entity corroboration (Wikidata→Wikipedia→Crunchbase→G2/Clutch hierarchy), research amplification, and a vertical-by-product map.
- **4 phases, sequenced by impact:** (1) AI-citeable content + extractability + tooling; (2) entity & knowledge graph; (3) earned off-site authority + referral activation; (4) score polish + compounding. Plus a 12-month month-by-month roadmap.
- **Projected outcomes** tables: CITE progression, scenario planning (A +8.25→70 / B +11→81 / C +12.5→93), SEO/traffic, AI/GEO, conversion-rate levers, content-asset ROI.
- **Product Page CITE Template** (reusable per-product checklist) — asset audit + 7 page-level fixes with `[product]`/`[primary keyword]` placeholders; teams apply to their own product pages.
- **Benchmark examples:** HubSpot (research → citations), Appinventiv (800+ Clutch reviews → badge), Ahrefs (Wikidata/Wikipedia → first AI-cited source).
- **Two clocks:** retrieval-time citations move in weeks–months (Phases 1–2); parametric over years (Phases 3–4).
- **⚠️ Data conflicts flagged for resolution before I-dimension work:** founding year (audit 2015 vs 2016), HQ city (Madurai vs Chennai), portfolio size (200+ clients / 100+ countries vs 750+ projects) — these feed Wikidata/NAP/schema and must be reconciled first.
