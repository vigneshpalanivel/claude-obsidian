## Key Facts Extracted

- **Target 90+ (Excellent)** from 62 baseline; explicitly framed as a 12–18 month program, with ~75 (Good) achievable in one quarter.
- **Guiding principle:** earn the score, don't game it — reject any tactic that lifts the number without producing real authority.
- **Per-dimension conversion roadmap:** each of the 40 items mapped to what flips it to Pass, with an **AI Citation Impact** column (High/Med/Low/None).
- **AI-citation impact is a separate axis from score:** cheap points (copyright T08, cookie banner T04, NAP I03, social links I05) are rated None for citations.
- **Highest AI-citation levers:** original research (E05, "Highest"), press (E04), answer-engine content (C05–C08, E06), entity/KG (I01, I10), retrievability (E01).
- **4 phases, sequenced by impact:** (1) AI-citeable content + extractability + tooling; (2) entity & knowledge graph; (3) earned off-site authority; (4) score polish + compounding.
- **Two clocks:** retrieval-time citations move in weeks–months (Phases 1–2); parametric citations over years (Phases 3–4).
- Tooling (Ahrefs/Semrush + AI-citation monitoring) moved to a Phase 1 task — required to confirm citation movement.
