## Key Facts Extracted

- Core thesis: informational keywords are now AI territory; shift focus to BoFU (hire, compare, pricing, best agency for).
- Three-step process: Diagnose (GSC impressions vs clicks) → Optimize for AIO → GEO (multi-platform).
- Answer-first structure: put the answer in the first 2-3 sentences; AI extracts earliest clear answer.
- E-E-A-T is now universal (not just YMYL): Experience, Expertise, Authoritativeness, Trustworthiness.
- AI crawlers must be explicitly allowed in robots.txt: GPTBot, Google-Extended, PerplexityBot.
- Semantic HTML required; AI doesn't execute JavaScript — JS-rendered content is invisible.
- GEO platform split: Google AIO (schema + E-E-A-T), ChatGPT (brand mentions), Perplexity (original research), Gemini (YouTube + GSC), Claude/others (structured facts).
- Three content types that still drive clicks: original data/research, interactive tools, expert POV.
- New metrics: AI citation rate, LLM referral traffic (GA4), share of voice, conversions.
- CITE score off-page framework (target 80): Editorial Links (HARO), Anchor Text Mix (40-50% branded, <5% exact match), Knowledge Graph (Wikipedia/Wikidata), Review Platforms (Clutch, GoodFirms, G2, CrunchBase).
