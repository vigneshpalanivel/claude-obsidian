## Key Facts Extracted

- 6 diagnosed weak points: sales culture, stagnant product features, no growth orientation, quality issues, profit pressure under financial stress, leads volume.
- Growth framework maps to all 4 Ansoff quadrants; Market Penetration (clone SEO + referrals) and Market Development (industry keywords) are near-term.
- 10% quarterly growth target set as minimum across all departments.
- Sales team restructured into 2 stages: Leads-to-Opportunity and Opportunity-to-Sale.
- 6-criteria opportunity qualification system: needs, intent, budget, demo, responsiveness, referral.
- Default entry price: $999; Makent floor $999 with mobile app mandatory.
- Monthly big-deal target: at least one deal around $5K.
- 14 OKR KPIs tracked for sales; focus on conversion ratios and engagement rates.
- Primary process gaps: CRM hygiene, follow-up cadence, demo quality, product knowledge within the sales team.
