# ICP — InnBlockchain Channel Partner (Blockchain Advisory Firms)

Source file: `.raw/InnBlockchain - ICP - Channel Partner-I.md` (the "-I" distinguishes it from Trioangle's channel partner ICP).

Internal ICP document for InnBlockchain's advisory-firm channel partners. See [[InnBlockchain Channel Partner ICP]] for the synthesized concept page.

> [!WARNING] Validation status — assumption-based. Built from first-principles reasoning and cross-reference with InnBlockchain Crypto Native, FinTech, and the Trioangle Channel Partner ICP. Ranges and severities are hypotheses until partner interviews and deals validate them.

## Pitch Angle

InnBlockchain is positioned as a blockchain-native development and smart contract security partner. Advisory firms sub-contract the build phase — end-to-end or specific components (smart contracts, audit, backend, frontend) — and **retain full ownership of the client relationship and billing**.

## Personas

1. **Web3 Strategy Consultant** — boutique 2–15 person Web3 advisory/tokenomics firm, zero engineers, crypto-native identity. Budget: full authority; client builds $20k–$300k+.
2. **FinTech Digital Transformation Consultant** — advises regulated FinTechs on blockchain strategy, MiCA compliance, tokenization. Driven by regulatory deadlines.
3. **Crypto GTM & Tokenomics Advisor** — solo/small-team, token launch focus. **Primary motivator: personal reputational protection, not recurring revenue.** One vesting contract failure ends the career.

## Key Pain Points

- "We do the hard advisory work and lose the implementation contract" (10/10)
- Unreliable freelance Solidity devs as client relationship risk (9/10)
- Smart contract security is outside our expertise (9/10)
- Can't scale advisory to include implementation without hiring (8/10)
- Scoping blockchain builds is impossible without a technical partner (7/10)
- FinTech clients need blockchain + compliance but they're two separate problems (8/10, P2-specific)

## Routing Hub

This ICP is the hub that routes end-clients across InnBlockchain's other three ICPs:

- Blockchain build $20k+ (crypto-native) → **[[InnBlockchain Crypto Native ICP]]**
- Blockchain build $20k+ (regulated FinTech) → **[[InnBlockchain FinTech ICP]]**
- Sub-$20k crypto-native → **[[InnBlockchain Crypto Products ICP]]** (Trioangle clone script, $5k–$20k, 5–15 days)
- Sub-$20k FinTech → scope smaller or park (no Crypto Products routing)
- Non-blockchain brief → **Trioangle** (see [[Trioangle Channel Partner Personas]])

## Discovery Sprint

$1,500–$4,000 paid pre-sales scoping for all partner types — mirrors Trioangle channel partner playbook.
