# Content Strategy — Trioangle Clone Script Blog

**Source**: `.raw/Trioangle - Content Strategy - Product.md` | Trioangle internal | 2026 | Status: in-progress

---

## What This Document Is

Trioangle's blog content strategy for the **product line** (clone scripts and super-app platforms). Targets [[Trioangle Buyer Personas]] (P1-P5/P6). Distinct from [[Content-Strategy-Service]], which covers the custom development service line.

Goal: capture high-intent organic search traffic, build topical authority, and nurture leads through a 2-10 week research-heavy buying cycle.

| # | Pillar | Funnel Stage | Primary Pain Point | Personas |

| 1 | Startup Launchpad | TOFU/MOFU | Idea-to-Execution Chasm | P1, P3 |

| 5 | Customer Success & Social Proof | BOFU | Trust Deficit (Burned Builder) | P2, P3 |

> Pillar 5 is the most critical for Persona 2 (Burned Builder). Every piece in this pillar must convert skepticism - video testimonials, refund policy, ability to speak with a past buyer directly.

## Vertical-Specific Notes

**Ride-Hailing**: Highest search volume. Primary: North America, UK, EU, Middle East. Secondary: Africa, Southeast Asia, Latin America.

**Creator Economy / Short Video**: TikTok clone (PopTok) generating inbound. Younger founders (22-32), strong organic demand. Prioritize immediately.

**Multi-Service Super App**: Primary persona is Persona 5 (Super-App Ecosystem Builder) — highest LTV buyer. Content must validate the expansion ambition and address "can a clone handle this?" fear. Full proof content must exist before their 30-60 day research cycle completes.

**Home & Handyman Services**: Primary persona is Persona 4 (Small Business Digitalizer). Content must emphasise ROI, operational continuity, offline-to-digital transition — NOT startup founding. T1 ("How to Start...") is for Persona 1 framing. Use T14 trigger variant for Persona 4.

**Dating Apps (8b)**: Treated as a separate content silo. Primary concern: cultural relevance and network-effect strategy.

## Article Template System

20 templates (T1-T20) + 8 lead magnets (L1-L8). Full specs in [[Product Blog Article Templates]].

**Variable rule**: Use `[ORIGINAL]` (Uber, Airbnb, TikTok) in article titles and keywords — searchers look for "Uber clone script", not "Gofer clone script". Use `[BRAND]` only in CTAs and post-purchase content. Use `[COMPETITOR]` (Appdupe, V3Cube) only in T9 comparison articles.

| Stage | Templates |
|-------|-----------|
| TOFU (Awareness) | T1, T2, T3, T14 |
| MOFU (Consideration) | T4, T5, T6, T7, T8 |
| BOFU (Decision) | T9, T10, T11 |
| Post-Purchase | T12, T13 |
| Cross-Vertical | T14, T15, T16, T17, T18, T19 |
| Per-Vertical Recurring | T20 (8 articles — one per supported vertical, for diaspora persona) |

**Standout templates:**
- **T18**: "Clone Script vs AI Builders (Cursor, Lovable): Why Prompting Isn't a Product" — directly addresses the Persona 6 (AI Builder Who Hit the Wall) buying context and the objection from all personas about building with AI tools instead.
- **T14 Trigger variant**: "Regulatory Gap Window — Launch First" — captures the emerging market opportunity window (TikTok restrictions, WhatsApp bans, etc.)
