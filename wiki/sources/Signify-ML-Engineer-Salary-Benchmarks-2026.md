# Signify Technology: ML Engineer Salary Benchmarks 2025-2026 (US)

**Source**: [signifytechnology.com](https://www.signifytechnology.com/news/machine-learning-engineer-salary-benchmarks-us-market-2025-2026/) | Signify Technology | 2026

---

## Summary

US salary data for ML engineers 2025-2026. Demand-to-supply ratio of 3.2:1 — hiring remains the primary bottleneck for enterprise ML.

---

## Salary Bands (US, base only)

| Level | Range |
|-------|-------|
| Junior (0-2 yrs) | $100K–$165K |
| Mid-Level (3-5 yrs) | $145K–$220K |
| Senior (6-9 yrs) | $195K–$275K |
| Lead/Principal (10+ yrs) | $208K–$355K |

---

## Regional Differences

- **SF Bay Area** — highest; Lead reaches $355K base
- **NYC** — Lead $240K–$320K
- **Seattle, Austin, US remote** — 10–15% below Bay Area

## Industry Differences

- **Hedge funds / quant** — $300K–$500K+ for senior
- **FAANG** — $320K–$550K total comp
- **Startups, healthcare** — 15–25% below top tier

---

## Specialization Premiums (additional over baseline)

| Specialization | Premium |
|----------------|---------|
| Generative AI / LLMs | +40-60% ($56K-$110K) |
| MLOps | +25-40% ($35K-$74K) |
| NLP | +20-35% ($28K-$64K) |
| Rust for ML | +15-20% ($21K-$37K) |
| PyTorch | +8-12% ($10K-$22K) |

---

## Total Compensation

Senior ML engineer total annual value (base + equity + benefits): **$322,750 to $521,400**.

---

## Market Reality

- Demand-to-supply ratio **3.2:1** — talent shortage is severe
- AI/ML job postings up **89% H1 2025**
- 40% projected growth in AI specialist roles through 2030

> [!gap] US-only data. India/SEA ML engineer rates differ significantly — likely 10-20% of US numbers for equivalent seniority.
