# Content Strategy — InnBlockchain Crypto Products

Source: `.raw/InnBlockchain - Content Strategy - Crypto Products.md`

## Summary

Content strategy for the Trioangle-branded crypto clone script product line ($5k-$25k). 4 verticals × 5 pillars × 40 scored articles × 5 personas. The defining content dynamic: **claims mean nothing, verifiable on-chain proof is everything**. Security Hub publishes first as universal trust foundation; all other content links back to it.

## Verticals & Products

| Vertical | Products | Persona Primary | Search Tier |
|----------|----------|-----------------|-------------|
| 🏦 CEX | Binance/Coinbase/Bybit clones | Regional Market Pioneer + Burned Builder | HIGH (highest vertical demand) |
| 🤝 P2P | Remitano/Paxful/LocalBitcoins clones | Diaspora Fintech Builder | HIGH |
| 🔄 DEX/DeFi | Uniswap/PancakeSwap/GMX/dYdX/1inch clones, yield farming | Web3 Protocol Architect | MEDIUM-HIGH (lower volume, higher value) |
| 🎨 NFT | OpenSea/Rarible clones, GameFi NFT | Community Platform Monetizer | MEDIUM |

## Content Pillars

1. **🚀 Exchange Launchpad & Tech Infrastructure** (TOFU→MOFU) — Pain Point 1 (Exchange Infrastructure Complexity) + 5 (Blockchain/Wallet Integration)
2. **🔐 Security & Smart Contract Safety** (TOFU→MOFU) — **#1 purchase blocker for every persona; the credibility layer that gates all conversion**
3. **⚖️ Compliance & Regulatory Navigator** (MOFU) — MiCA, FCA, DIFC/ADGM, SEC/FinCEN for priority markets
4. **💧 Liquidity & Growth Playbook** (MOFU→Post-Purchase) — bootstrap, LP incentives, market makers
5. **⭐ Proof & On-Chain Credibility** (BOFU) — case studies, on-chain addresses, Trioangle vs B2Broker/AlphaPoint/Hayvn/Rain/Shift Markets

## Persona Distribution Rules

- **Persona 2 (Diaspora Builder)**: Facebook diaspora groups + WhatsApp communities are primary channels. Search/Twitter are secondary. Peer referral is the trust mechanism.
- **Persona 3 (Web3 Protocol Architect)**: technical precision or trust is destroyed instantly. Lead with audit docs + on-chain TVL proof. Blog alone does not convert — sales must offer NDA code access.
- **Persona 5 (Burned Crypto Builder)**: longest cycle (4-8 weeks). #37 Restart Guide catches at TOFU. Reddit (r/defi, r/CryptoCurrency) + Crypto Twitter are discovery channels.
- **Priority market buyers (US/UK/EU/ME)**: AI Search (ChatGPT, Perplexity, Gemini) is first research step before Google. Content must be citation-structured.

## Format Mix

25% Technical Guides · 20% Security/Proof · 20% How-To Launchpad · 15% Comparison/Buyer Guides · 15% Case Studies · 5% Industry Reports.

Tone rule: **never write "easy" or "simple" about smart contract security**. Every claim needs verifiable proof.
