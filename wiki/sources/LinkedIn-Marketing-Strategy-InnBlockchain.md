# LinkedIn Marketing Strategy — InnBlockchain

Source: `.raw/InnBlockchain - LinkedIn Marketing Strategy.md`

## Summary

Platform execution strategy for LinkedIn as InnBlockchain's primary B2B channel. Covers profile setup (Creator Mode, Showcase Pages, Lead Gen Forms), algorithm mechanics, content cadence and formats, authority-building (newsletter, strategic comments, social proof), and analytics tracking. Two ICPs served from the same profile: regulated FinTech (Sam, Tom) and crypto-native (Felix, Sasha). EU Continental Europe sole geography until 5-project gate met.

## Platform Setup

### Personal Profile (Vignesh)
- **Creator Mode**: On (mandatory — unlocks analytics, follower count, featured links, "Follow" CTA)
- **Custom URL**: `linkedin.com/in/vigneshpalanivel`
- **Open to**: "Providing services" (surfaces in service provider search)
- **Profile language**: English only

### Company Page (InnBlockchain)
- **Showcase Pages to create**: Smart Contract Security · FinTech Blockchain Engineering · RWA Tokenization
- **Lead Gen Forms**: Enabled (captures name, email, company, job title without leaving LinkedIn)
- **CTA button**: "Contact us" → discovery call booking page

## Algorithm Mechanics

### Ranking Signals (in order)
1. Early engagement velocity — first 60-90 minutes are the strongest signal
2. Comments over reactions — 3-5x the weight of a like
3. Dwell time — long-form carousels and multi-paragraph text outperform short posts
4. 1st-degree connection proximity — ICP-aligned network = direct organic reach
5. Keyword relevance — use ICP keywords (MiCA, RWA, Solidity, smart contract audit) in every post

### What Gets Penalised
- External links in post body (put links in first comment)
- Tagging non-engaging accounts
- Low early engagement — delete and repost rather than leaving a decaying post
- 10+ hashtags (3-5 is optimal)

### Optimal Posting Times (EU / CET)

| Day | Window | Rationale |
|-----|--------|-----------|
| Tuesday | 08:00-09:30 | Post-Monday momentum, high engagement |
| Wednesday | 09:00-10:30 | Peak mid-week |
| Thursday | 08:00-10:00 | Decision-maker activity spike |
| Monday | 07:30-09:00 | Morning feed check |
| Friday | Avoid | Low B2B engagement after 12:00 |

Alternate personal (Vignesh) and company page on different days — not the same day.

## Content Cadence

| Account | Frequency | Mix |
|---------|-----------|-----|
| Vignesh (personal) | 4-5x/week | 3x text posts · 1x carousel · 1x video or poll |
| InnBlockchain (company) | 3x/week | 2x repurposed Vignesh posts + 1x company-specific |

## Post Formats

| Format | Algorithm | Best Use |
|--------|-----------|----------|
| Text post | ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ | Thought leadership, takes, contrarian views (150-300 words) |
| Carousel (PDF) | ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ | Step-by-step guides, checklists (7-12 slides) |
| Short video (native) | ⭐⭐⭐⭐ | On-camera, trending takes (30-90s, captions mandatory) |
| Poll | ⭐⭐⭐⭐ | Engagement driver; audience research |
| LinkedIn Article | ⭐⭐⭐ | Long-form SEO authority (1,200-2,500 words) |

## Hook Formulas (First 2 Lines)

- **Counterintuitive claim**: "Most crypto exchanges get hacked not because of blockchain — it's poor API key management."
- **Specific number**: "3 smart contract patterns EU WealthTech platforms get wrong before a MiCA audit."
- **Fear consequence**: "One oracle exploit on an RWA platform doesn't drain a pool — it transfers someone's property."
- **Challenge belief**: "Passing a Slither audit doesn't mean your contracts are safe. Here's what it misses."

## Authority Building

### 5 Topic Clusters (3 of every 5 posts must be in these)

| Cluster | Primary ICP | Target Association |
|---------|------------|-------------------|
| MiCA compliance engineering | FinTech (Sam, Tom, Carol) | "Vignesh is the person to talk to about MiCA + blockchain" |
| RWA tokenization security | FinTech WealthTech + CN RWA Felix | "InnBlockchain builds RWA platforms that survive regulatory review" |
| Smart contract audit readiness | Crypto Native (Felix, Sasha) | "They know how to get a protocol audit-ready fast" |
| AI-generated smart contract risk | Both ICPs | "They flagged this problem before anyone else did" |
| Web3 talent gap solutions | Both ICPs (Sam, Paulo) | "They solve the engineering capacity problem without the hiring risk" |

### LinkedIn Newsletter: "Secure by Design"
- Bi-weekly, 400-700 words per issue, one topic per issue
- Alternate FinTech angle (MiCA, RWA, compliance) and Crypto Native angle (audit readiness, AI-code risk)
- Target: 500 subscribers in first 90 days
- Newsletter bypasses feed algorithm entirely — direct notification to every subscriber

### Strategic Comment Strategy
- 5-10 high-quality comments/day before publishing your own post
- Comment within 30 minutes of a post going live (early comments get most visibility)
- 3-5 sentences max; add specific point or data; end with a question when possible
- **Targets**: FinTech thought leaders (Finextra, Money20/20 speakers, MiCA commentators), Web3/DeFi voices, EU blockchain regulatory voices

### Engagement Tactics
- Drop external links in first comment, not post body
- Reply to every comment within the first hour (re-triggers algorithm)
- Tag sparingly — only when genuinely referenced and likely to engage
- Repost ICP-relevant news with 2-3 lines of analysis

## Analytics Targets

| Metric | Target | Cadence |
|--------|--------|---------|
| Profile Views | 100-300/week | Weekly |
| Search Appearances | Trending up week-on-week | Weekly |
| Post Impressions | 500-5,000+ per post | Per post |
| Engagement Rate | 2-5% | Per post |
| Follower Growth | +50-100/month | Monthly |
| Company Page Views | 200+/week | Weekly |

### Weekly Review Checklist
- Top 3 posts by impressions — what format/topic drove it?
- Profile view spike correlation to specific post
- Newsletter subscriber count growth
- Comment activity — any ICP-relevant accounts?
- Outreach response rates (see [[LinkedIn-Outreach-Strategy-InnBlockchain]])
- New qualified leads logged to Zoho CRM
- 1 post identified for carousel or video repurposing next week

## Geography Gate

EU Continental Europe is the **sole** active outbound geography for both FinTech and Crypto Native ICPs until 5 projects are closed. Do not run LinkedIn ABM sequences targeting UK, US, MENA, or SEA before that gate is met.

## Open Questions

- No clear mechanism defined for tracking which posts are driving newsletter subscribers vs. direct profile visits.
- "Delete and repost" for low-engagement posts needs a defined threshold (what qualifies as "no engagement in the first hour"?).
- Authority timeline is 60-90 days — no interim milestones defined between Day 1 and Day 60.
