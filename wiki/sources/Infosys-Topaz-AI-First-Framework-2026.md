# Infosys Topaz AI-First Value Framework 2026

## Scale & Scope
- **AI projects underway**: 4,600+
- **Top 200 clients working with on AI**: 90%
- **New service offerings built**: 30+ across six value pools
- **AI as % of quarterly revenue**: 5.5% (Q3 FY2026)
- **Market opportunity targeted**: $300–$400B incremental AI services by 2030

## Core Framework: AI-First Value
Two-pronged strategy:
1. **Capture new demand** for AI-first services across six value pools
2. **Augment existing services** with AI to expand wallet share

## Infosys Topaz Fabric (Launched 2025)
Composable stack of layered, interoperable components:
- Data infrastructure
- Foundation models
- AI agents
- AI flows and orchestration
- AI apps

Delivery modes: humans-in-the-loop agents, autonomous end-to-end workflow agents, human augmentation agents.

## Strategic Partnerships (2026)
- **OpenAI** — enterprise software development and modernization transformation
- **AWS** — combining Topaz with Amazon Q Developer for enterprise GenAI adoption
- **Google Cloud, Microsoft Azure** — multi-cloud AI delivery

## Competitive Claim
Infosys positions as "uniquely able to capture new AI services opportunity of over $300 billion" — framing its large workforce as advantage for human-in-loop AI delivery rather than a liability.

## Analyst Recognition
Named Leader in Inaugural AI Technical Services by Forrester Research, Q4 2025.
