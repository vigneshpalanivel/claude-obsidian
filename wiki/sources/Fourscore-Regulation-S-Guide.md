# Fourscore Business Law — Regulation S Offering Guide

## Source Summary

Practitioner-oriented guide aimed at U.S. emerging-companies counsel. Confirms Reg S framework but at a high level — no Cat 1/2/3 deep dive, no full US person definition. Useful as an orientation primer, not as a primary authority.

## What It Contributes

- Plain-English statement of the two core conditions (offshore transaction + no directed selling efforts)
- Confirmation that Reg S covers two scenarios: U.S. issuer raising offshore, or U.S. investor buying offshore securities through foreign markets
- Notes Reg S consists of 5 rules (901–905) with 903/904 doing the heavy lifting

## Limitations

- Does not define "U.S. person"
- Does not enumerate examples of directed selling efforts
- Does not explain category eligibility thresholds
