# Spinutech — Six Steps of End-to-End Content Production

## Summary

Operationally focused guide for running the full content production cycle. Highlights the content strategist as the glue holding all steps together and emphasizes that without a designated owner, workflows collapse.

## Six Steps

1. **Research upfront** — keyword research, content audits, internal team surveys, SEO/CRO collaboration
2. **Ideate and document** — organize ideas into documented strategy, outline goals, KPIs, publishing schedule
3. **Create content** — use research and outline tools, AI assistance for efficiency
4. **Distribute and manage** — share across marketing channels, update content inventory
5. **Measure performance** — monthly KPI analysis, trend identification, stakeholder reporting
6. **Optimize** — repurpose older assets, refresh with updated information

## What It Contributes

Strong on the operational mechanics — particularly the measurement-to-optimization loop and the role of the content strategist as a workflow coordinator, not just a strategist.
