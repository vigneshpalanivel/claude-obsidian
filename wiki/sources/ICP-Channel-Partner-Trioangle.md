# ICP — Trioangle Agencies & Consulting Firms (Channel Partner)

**Source**: `.raw/Trioangle - ICP - Channel Partner.md` | Trioangle internal | 2026 | Status: assumption-based, not yet validated

---

## What This Document Is

Trioangle's Ideal Channel Partner profile. These are not end-buyers of software; they are **intermediaries** (agencies and consulting firms) who bring client briefs to Trioangle for sub-contracted delivery. Distinct from [[ICP-Service-Trioangle]] (direct buyers) and [[ICP-Product-Trioangle]] (clone script buyers).

---

## Channel Partner ICP Summary

| Attribute | Details |
|---|---|
| **Who** | Digital Agencies, IT/Software Consulting, Business Management Consulting, Digital Marketing Agencies with product/UX practice |
| **Size** | 5-150 employees, $200k-$10M ARR; sweet spot: 10-50 employees |
| **Geography** | Tier 1: US, Canada, UK, UAE. Tier 2: Germany, Netherlands, France, Australia, Singapore. India excluded. |
| **Business model** | Retainer + Project. Bills end-clients $50-$200/hr or flat project fees. |
| **Delivery modes** | Three service lines (post-pivot): 🤖 AI Engineering ($15k-$120k+, often recurring 2-4x/year); ⚙️ Custom Dev ($10k-$120k+); 🛒 Clone Script ($500-$25k). **P1 Overflow Agency exception:** Clone Script is the primary route (end clients skew Product ICP). AI Engineering is the wedge against vibe-coding commoditization per [[Trioangle Growth Plan]]. |
| **Qualifying budget** | AI Engineering: $15k+ per package. Custom Dev: $10k+ net dev cost. Clone Script: $500+ **with a named live client brief** (bulk licensing disqualifying). |
| **Pricing model** | Project- and deliverable-based only. No retainers, AMC, or subscriptions. Volume incentives triggered by signed work. |

## Key Objection: Non-Circumvention

The primary trust barrier for all 3 personas. Response: NCNDA is a **signed contract**, not a verbal promise. Agency owns the client relationship; Trioangle operates as named or unnamed sub-contractor by the agency's choice.

> **Proactive framing note**: The "800+ projects" stat can raise the concern *"If you deliver directly, why do you need me?"* Address head-on: the partner model is permanent, not a stepping stone. Trioangle has no direct sales motion for these markets.

---

## Segment Access - Clients Faster to Reach via Partners

Two [[ICP-Service-Trioangle]] buyer types are **technically reachable** via Trioangle's direct outbound (LinkedIn ABM, Clutch, VC referrals are listed as active channels in the Service ICP), but conversion is slow and trust-gated. Channel partners compress the cycle from months to weeks:

- **Digital Transformation Enterprise Buyer** (Persona 3 in Service ICP) - Engages direct vendors primarily through formal RFP processes initiated by management consulting and Gartner/analyst relationships. Direct Clutch/LinkedIn rarely clears the procurement bar on its own.
- **MENA Relationship Buyer** (Persona 4 in Service ICP, budget $30k-$120k+) - Relationship-first. A warm introduction from a MENA-based consulting firm outweighs cold portfolio evidence by a wide margin.

Prioritise partner outreach to: management consulting firms (Enterprise Buyer access) and UAE/KSA-based digital agencies (MENA Buyer access).

## Two Scoping Sprints (Pre-Sales Tools)

Both are paid engagements at $1,500-$4,000 (1-2 weeks). Routing depends on brief type:

| Sprint | When to Route | Deliverables |
|---|---|---|
| **🤖 AI Audit Sprint** | AI-feature brief on existing platform (dynamic pricing, dispatch AI, demand forecasting, RAG, AI moderation, conversational UX) | AI opportunity assessment, data/integration feasibility, recommended approach, ROI projection, fixed-price quote |
| **⚙️ Discovery Sprint** | Greenfield Custom Dev or template-fit Clone Script | Requirements spec, architecture blueprint, ERD, wireframes, sprint plan, fixed-price quote |

AI Audit Sprint is the default routing for any brief from an AI-Forward Platform Operator end-client (existing platform + AI add-on requirement).

## Channel Priority (Top 3)

1. **LinkedIn ABM** - Sales Navigator targeting agency owners posting content in Trioangle-covered verticals
2. **Referral Program** - Existing Trioangle clients who run agencies; structured revenue share accelerates referrals
3. **Cold Email (Apollo/ZoomInfo)** - Target agency founders using job postings and vertical content as trigger signals

> Note: Clutch is NOT a primary inbound source for channel partners. Agencies vet vendors on Clutch after LinkedIn/cold contact. Optimize Clutch as a trust validator, not a lead source.

---

## Competitive Edge vs. Direct Competitors

Most direct competition: ValueCoders, Konstant Infosolutions, Appinventiv, Mindinventory (Indian dev firms targeting agencies). Trioangle's edge: formal NCNDA + partner program, named PM per engagement, publicly verifiable vertical portfolio (live apps to demo), 800+ domain-specific builds.

New threat (2026): AI coding tools (Cursor, Lovable) as agency-internal alternative to sub-contracting. Counter: AI generates scaffolding, not production-grade marketplace systems. Multi-role auth, real-time GPS dispatch, commission flows are not solved by code generation.

---

## "800+ Projects" Stat Guard

The "800+ projects across 150+ countries" figure is **cumulative-since-2015 historical delivery**, NOT active customers. Current active customer base is ~30 (April 2026). For Persona 2 Consulting Upsellers (who run reference checks) and Persona 3 Vertical Specialists (who demand domain proof), frame this as *"a decade of marketplace and on-demand delivery experience across 150+ countries"* rather than implying active pipeline. Reference lists must be backed by named, recent (12-month) accounts.

## Pricing Model — Project/Deliverable-Based Only

Trioangle does not offer retainers, AMC, or subscription tiers. All pricing is project- and deliverable-based. The Margin Model objection response previously promised "retainer arrangements" — corrected in Pass 3 to signed-volume preferred-partner pricing tiers (e.g., 4+ projects/year or aggregate annual billings). Aligns with [[Trioangle Growth Plan]] policy.
