# Trioangle — Product Verticals (Non-Crypto)

**Source**: `.raw/Trioangle - Product Verticals.md` | Trioangle internal | 2026
**Method**: Grouped from sitemap analysis of trioangle.com. Excludes all crypto/web3/blockchain products.

---

## Summary

Trioangle offers **~205 distinct product scripts** across 8 verticals. This is the product catalog — distinct from the **800+ deployments** figure (which counts live businesses launched by buyers). The 205 covers unique script types; buyers launch one or more of these.
