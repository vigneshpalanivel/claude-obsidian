## Why Tokenization Matters for Fintech

1. **Liquidity unlocking**: Illiquid assets (real estate, private credit) become tradeable 24/7
2. **Fractional ownership**: Lowers minimum investment thresholds; democratizes access
3. **Settlement efficiency**: T+0 vs T+2 for traditional securities
4. **Programmability**: Smart contracts automate dividends, compliance, corporate actions
5. **Audit trail**: Immutable ownership record reduces custodian fraud risk

## Regulatory Momentum

- More regulatory clarity → more institutional inflows → more liquidity → more regulation (virtuous cycle)
- EU MiCA framework and US digital asset guidelines clarifying tokenized securities treatment
- RWA sector growing faster than any other crypto sector as of 2026

## Top Blockchains for RWA

Ethereum dominates; Solana, Stellar, and Avalanche growing in institutional RWA use. R3 Corda planning RWA yield vault on Solana (H1 2026).
