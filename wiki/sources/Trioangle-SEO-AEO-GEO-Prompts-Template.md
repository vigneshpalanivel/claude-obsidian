# 100+ SEO + AEO + GEO Prompts Template — Trioangle

**Source**: `.raw/Trioangle/sales-marketing/Service/SEO/SEO_AEO_GEO_Prompts_Template.md` | Trioangle internal | 2026

---

## What This Document Is

A reusable 180-prompt master template that operationalises the [[SEO-Recovery-Strategy-2025-26]] for every Trioangle clone script product. The template carries placeholders ([Product Name], [Company Name], [Script Type], etc.) that get substituted per product instance — Gofer, Makent, GoferEats, PopTok, Igniter, etc. — to generate a complete keyword + answer + AI-citation content map for that vertical.

Pairs with [[Product-Blog-Article-Templates]]: the 20-template article briefs are the "what to write," while this prompts library is the "what to rank/answer/cite for."

---

## Structure at a Glance

| Section | Range | Count | Engine | Output Format |
|---------|-------|-------|--------|---------------|
| **SEO** | 1-75 | 75 | Google / Bing rankings | Blog posts, H1/H2, meta titles, FAQ schema |
| **AEO** | 76-115 | 40 | Featured snippets, voice search | 40-60 word answers + FAQPage JSON-LD |
| **GEO** | 116-175 | 60 | ChatGPT, Gemini, Claude, Perplexity, Bing Copilot | 1,500-3,000 word authority content |
| **Hybrid** | 176-180 | 5 | All three simultaneously | Pillar pages |

## Format Rules by Engine

**SEO (Section 1):**
- Use as blog post titles, H1/H2 headings, meta titles
- FAQ schema entries on landing pages
- Pillar + cluster content planning

**AEO (Section 2):**
- 40-60 word direct response
- Definition-first paragraph (lead sentence answers the query)
- FAQPage JSON-LD on every page
- Natural voice-search phrasing ("How do I…", "Can I…")

**GEO (Section 3):**
- 1,500-3,000 word long-form authoritative content
- Embed brand citations: "N+ projects, N+ countries"
- Organization + Product + FAQ schema
- Publish on high-authority domains for AI training citation
- Press releases, guest posts, industry directories
- Anticipate follow-up questions AI models expect

|----------|------|---------------|----------------|
| 🔴 High | AEO | Google Snippets | FAQ + Definition |
| 🔴 High | GEO | ChatGPT / Gemini | Long-form Article |

| 🟡 Medium | SEO | Google / Bing | Landing Page |
| 🟡 Medium | AEO | Voice / Snippets | How-To Guide |

The matrix doubles as a sequencing guide: AEO + GEO Hybrid (176-180) ship first as pillar pages, then High-priority AEO definitions, then BoFU SEO comparison/transactional, then long-tail.

## Why This Matters Strategically

1. **Operationalises the GEO/AIO pivot** in [[SEO-Recovery-Strategy-2025-26]]. The strategy says "info keywords are AI territory; shift to BoFU and AI citation" — this template enumerates exactly which 180 prompts to attack.
2. **Multiplies cleanly across all 10 product verticals**. One template instance × 10 verticals = ~1,800 prompt-targeted assets without redesigning the schema.
3. **Aligns with E-E-A-T trust signals** the AI engines use to decide what to cite. GEO sub-buckets 126-135 (Contextual Authority) explicitly frame Trioangle as the expert via embedded "N+ countries", "N+ projects" trust statements.
4. **Bridges to [[Product-Blog-Article-Templates]]**. T-templates (T1-T20) tell writers the brief; these prompts tell SEO/AEO/GEO planners the targets. Same persona system (P1-P6 from [[Trioangle Buyer Personas]]).

The keyword + answer + citation map for every Trioangle clone script product. 1. Substitute placeholders from the product brand kit
2. Run prompts 1-40 (SEO Informational) → blog cluster
3. Run prompts 76-90 (AEO Definition) → FAQ schema set
4. Run prompts 116-135 (GEO Overview + Authority) → 5 long-form pillar articles
5. Wire prompts 176-180 (Hybrid) into the pillar landing page
