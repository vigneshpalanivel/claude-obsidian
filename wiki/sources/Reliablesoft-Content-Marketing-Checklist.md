# Reliablesoft — Complete Content Marketing Checklist

## Summary

Practical 10-step checklist covering the full content marketing lifecycle. Particularly strong on the planning and promotion phases with concrete sub-task lists.

## 10-Step Checklist

1. Learn content marketing basics
2. Know your audience (research, demographics, personas)
3. Devise a content marketing strategy (goals, tools, budget, frequency, channels)
4. Find content topic ideas (keyword targeting, format-specific lists)
5. Prepare an action plan (dates, channels, titles, keywords, responsibilities, promotion plan, review schedule)
6. Create diverse content types (pillar pages, topic clusters, evergreen content)
7. Optimize for search (titles, URLs, headings, ALT text, schema markup)
8. Promote content (email, social, backlinks, paid ads, internal links)
9. Use content marketing tools (Semrush, Ahrefs, GA, GSC, Grammarly)
10. Analyze campaign performance (visits, rankings, conversions, content audits)

## What It Contributes

Most actionable checklist structure — maps cleanly to a task list format. Redistribution cadence (14/30/90/120 days) is a concrete operational detail not found in other sources.
