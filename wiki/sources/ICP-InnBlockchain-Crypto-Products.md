# ICP — InnBlockchain Crypto Products

Source file: `.raw/InnBlockchain - ICP - Crypto Products.md`

Internal ICP document for the clone script product line. Note: although filed under InnBlockchain, the product is operated under the **[[Trioangle]]** brand. See [[InnBlockchain Crypto Products ICP]] for the synthesized concept page.

## Products

- **CEX clone** (Binance/Coinbase-style) — $8k–$20k
- **P2P clone** (Paxful/Remitano-style) — $5k–$12k
- **DEX/DeFi clone** (Uniswap/Pancakeswap-style)
- **NFT marketplace clone** (OpenSea/Rarible-style)

All live in **5–15 business days** vs. 3–6 months custom build.

## Archetype-First Persona Framework

Personas are defined by **who the buyer emotionally is**, not which product they intend to buy. The sales team identifies the archetype first, then matches to the product. Direct mapping to [[Trioangle Buyer Personas]]:

| Archetype | Trioangle Product ICP equivalent | Primary Products |
|---|---|---|
| Regional Market Pioneer | Visionary Velocity Founder | CEX, P2P |
| Diaspora Fintech Builder | Diaspora Digital Entrepreneur | P2P, CEX |
| Web3 Protocol Architect | *(unique — no equivalent)* | DEX / DeFi |
| Community Platform Monetizer | Small Business Digitalizer | NFT marketplace |
| **Burned Crypto Builder** | Burned Builder Comeback Founder | Any — trust is the lens |

## Key Takeaways

- **Burned Builder is the highest-urgency archetype.** Second-attempt buyers with validated market, capital, and forensic vendor evaluation. Closes faster than first-timers. Intro framing: *"This team has deployed 800+ platforms — here are on-chain addresses to verify before you commit a dollar."*
- **Priority markets** (North America, UK, EU, Middle East) are capital-rich, compliance-aware buyers with high intent. Secondary markets are still active but lower priority.
- **Security is a non-negotiable purchase blocker** — buyers will demand audit reports, KYC/AML walkthrough, cold storage architecture, and on-chain transaction records before purchase.
- **Diaspora Builder budgets are higher** than local Regional Pioneers ($8k–$18k) — funded from stable Western income.
- **Routing IN**: sub-$20k crypto-native end-clients from [[InnBlockchain Channel Partner ICP]] and [[InnBlockchain Crypto Native ICP]] land here. **Not** a valid destination for sub-$20k FinTech clients (clone scripts are designed for crypto-native founders).
- **Routing OUT**: re-engagement path at 60/90 days post-launch feeds back into InnBlockchain custom development when clients hit feature limits, close funding, or need regulatory licensing.
