- `[COMPETITOR]` = actual Trioangle vendor rivals (Appdupe, V3Cube) — only in T9 comparison articles
- Use primary `[ORIGINAL]` for first article per vertical; use alternates for additional articles targeting different markets

## Key Templates to Prioritise

**T18 — Clone Script vs AI Builders**: This template directly addresses the cross-cutting objection across all personas — "why not just use Cursor/Lovable?" Bridges the [[Vibe Coding]] wave and the [[Trioangle Buyer Personas]] Persona 6 (AI Builder Who Hit the Wall). Build early.

**T14 — Trigger Moment Entries**: Six situational variants capture high-urgency buyers who arrive via specific events (competitor launched, TikTok restrictions created a window, manual ops breaking). Each variant is a separate TOFU article.

**T11 — Case Studies**: For the Burned Builder persona, one strong case study in their vertical/geography outweighs all other content. These should be vertical × country specific (e.g., "Nigerian Founder Launched a Ride-Hailing App in 14 Days").
