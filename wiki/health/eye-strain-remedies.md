# Eye Strain — Prevention & Remedies

## Root Cause

Normal blink rate: 15–20 times/minute
During screen work: drops to 5–7 times/minute

Reduced blinking → dry eyes → eye strain → forehead headache accumulating through the day. AC office + allergic conjunctivitis (connected to sinusitis) amplifies this further.

**Why headache peaks in afternoon:** Eye strain is cumulative — 4–5 hours of screen work crosses the threshold into pain around early afternoon. Not a sudden trigger like gastric gas — a slow buildup.

---

## Daily Routine

| Time | Action |
|------|--------|
| Start of work | 1 drop lubricating eye drops each eye |
| Every 20 min | 20-20-20 rule |
| After lunch | 1 drop lubricating eye drops |
| Midday | Splash cold water on eyes |
| Every hour | Conscious full blink — 10 times slowly |
| After 5 PM | Night mode on all screens |

---

## 20-20-20 Rule

Every **20 minutes** → look at something **20 feet away** → for **20 seconds**

- Relaxes eye accommodation muscles
- Increases blink rate temporarily
- Rehydrates eye surface
- Set Break Timer app (free) to automate — runs silently, vibrates every 20 min

---

## Screen Setup (One-Time Fix)

| Factor | Correct | Wrong |
|--------|---------|-------|
| Distance | Arm's length (~60cm) | Too close or too far |
| Height | Top of monitor at eye level | Looking down at laptop |
| Brightness | Match room light | Bright screen in dim room |
| Night mode | On after 5 PM | Blue light all evening |
| Font size | Comfortable without squinting | Too small = constant strain |

> Looking **down** at a laptop strains both eyes AND neck — referred pain reaches forehead. Raising screen to eye level fixes eye and neck tension simultaneously.

## Eye Drops

**Lubricating eye drops (artificial tears):**
- OTC — no prescription needed
- Brands: **Refresh Tears** or **Systane** — available at any pharmacy in Madurai
- Use 2–3 times daily — prevention, not just when irritated
- Apply before starting work and after lunch

**Do not use:**
- Redness-relief drops (Visine type) — only masks symptoms, worsens dryness long term
- Any steroid drops without prescription

## Complete Allergic Response Pattern

All symptoms below are **one single allergic reaction** — not separate problems.

| Symptom | Cause |
|---------|-------|
| Body heat sensation | Histamine → blood vessel dilation |
| Sneezing | Histamine → nasal irritation |
| Eye itching | Histamine → conjunctival irritation |
| White eye discharge | Histamine → mucus production in eyes |
| Forehead pressure/headache | Sinus inflammation from same histamine response |

**Root cause chain:**
```
ALLERGY
    ↓
Histamine release
    ↓
Body heat + Sneezing + Eye itch + White discharge + Sinus inflammation
    ↓
Headache
```

### Common Triggers
- Dust — especially AC filters in office
- Temperature change — stepping from hot outside into cold AC
- Stress — directly releases histamine even without external allergen
- Early morning — histamine naturally peaks between 6–8 AM

### Medical Term
**Allergic rhinoconjunctivitis with sinusitis** — nose and eyes affected simultaneously by same allergen. Called "united airways."

## White Eye Discharge + Eye Itching

Both caused by histamine response — same allergy triggering nose and eyes simultaneously.

**Two types of eye drops needed:**

| Type | Purpose | Brand |
|------|---------|-------|
| **Antihistamine eye drops** | Stops itching at source | Ketotifen — OTC available |
| **Lubricating eye drops** | Relieves dryness | Refresh Tears or Systane |

- Plain lubricating drops help dryness but **will not stop histamine-driven itching**
- Ketotifen eye drops target the itch directly — available OTC at any pharmacy in Madurai
- Ask ENT for prescription antiallergic eye drops at appointment

**Sit away from direct AC vent** — single most effective environmental fix for office allergen exposure.

## Conscious Blinking

Most underrated fix — completely free, works immediately.

During intense screen focus, blinking becomes incomplete (half blinks) or stops entirely. This dries the eye surface within minutes.

**Practice:** Every hour, blink 10 times slowly and fully — let eyes close completely each time. Takes 15 seconds. Resets the tear film completely.

---

## Midday Eye Reset (2 minutes)

1. Close eyes for 30 seconds — complete darkness
2. Splash cold water on closed eyes — 5–6 splashes
3. Gently massage eyelids with clean fingertips — circular motion
4. Open eyes slowly

Do this at lunch break. Gives eyes a proper rest mid-session before the afternoon screen hours begin.

## Bilateral Temple Pain (Sides of Forehead Near Eyes)

**Diagnosis:** Tension-type headache — confirmed by Dr. S N Karthik (Sep 2024)

**Cause:** Temporalis muscle running along both sides of forehead tightens under stress and screen work → bilateral squeezing pain near eyes. NOT sinusitis — CT confirmed frontal sinuses are completely clear.

**Why massage gives temporary relief:** Releases temporalis muscle tension + increases blood flow. Returns within 30–60 min because underlying cause (stress, posture, screen) remains.

### Immediate Relief
| Fix | Method |
|-----|--------|
| Temporalis massage | Circular pressure on both temples with warm coconut or sesame oil — 2–3 min |
| Hot compress | Warm cloth on both temples — 5–10 min |
| Screen break | Stop screen immediately — ongoing trigger |
| Propranolol (Norten 10mg) | Already prescribed by Dr. Sabrin — take every morning consistently |

### Prevention
- Neck + shoulder release midday — tension originates in neck, refers to temples
- Propranolol daily morning — prescribed specifically for tension/migraine prevention
- Reduce stress load — cortisol directly tightens temporalis muscle

---

## Neck Stretch — Connected Fix

Forehead headache from eye strain is often worsened by neck and shoulder tension from screen posture. Both refer pain to the same location.

**2-minute midday neck routine:**
- Neck rolls — slow, full circle, 5 each direction
- Chin to chest hold — 10 seconds
- Ear to shoulder — 10 seconds each side
- Shoulder rolls — 10 forward, 10 backward

Do alongside the midday eye reset at lunch break.
