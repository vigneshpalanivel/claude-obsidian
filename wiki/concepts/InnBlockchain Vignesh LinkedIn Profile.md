# InnBlockchain Vignesh LinkedIn Profile

ICP-aligned rewrite of Vignesh's personal LinkedIn profile. Designed to support trigger-based outreach to **FinTech RWA/WealthTech** and **Crypto-Native RWA + DeFi/Protocol** ICP leads, per [[InnBlockchain LinkedIn Strategy]].

Source draft: `.raw/InnBlockchain/LinkedIn/LinkedIn Profile Vignesh Content.md` (replaces it).

## Why This Rewrite Was Needed

The original draft had five structural issues against the ICPs:

1. **"I don't compete on speed" undermined the core competitive advantage.** Documented Crypto Native objection handling reframes speed as: *Trail of Bits/OpenZeppelin queues run 6-12 weeks; we can start next week.* Felix and Sasha land on the profile with an audit deadline burning — the original line routed them to a faster firm.
2. **RWA was buried.** Both ICPs name RWA as Priority 1 ([[InnBlockchain LinkedIn Strategy]]). The original headlines placed MiCA, DeFi, or FinTech ahead of it.
3. **RWA Felix (asset-backed, non-technical) was invisible.** The Crypto Native ICP carves out real-estate developers, fleet owners, commodity producers, private-credit issuers. The original CTA only addressed "FinTech leaders" and "protocol founders" — excluding domain-expert founders.
4. **Compliance Carol got nothing.** Carol is the regulatory veto-holder who reads profiles during vendor risk assessment. Original profile had no vendor-risk-pack, ISO/SOC 2, or regulator-grade language.
5. **Agency-tone "helping... build" phrasing.** Vignesh sells infrastructure to 50-500-employee regulated FinTechs and $2M+ funded protocols — they don't buy from "helpers."
