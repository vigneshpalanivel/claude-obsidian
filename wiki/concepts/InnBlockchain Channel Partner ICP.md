# InnBlockchain Channel Partner ICP

Target **Web3 Strategy Consultancies, Blockchain Advisory Firms, FinTech Digital Transformation Consultancies, and Tokenomics/GTM Agencies** that advise crypto founders, DAOs, regulated FinTechs, and enterprise clients — but lack smart contract engineering, dApp development, or blockchain integration capability. These partners bring a recurring pipeline of clients moving from strategy into build. InnBlockchain becomes their silent delivery arm so they capture the full engagement lifecycle without hiring a single blockchain engineer.

> [!WARNING] Validation status — assumption-based. Ranges, severities, channel scores are hypotheses until partner interviews and deals validate them.

## Firmographics

- **Industry:** Web3 strategy consultancies, tokenomics advisory, blockchain GTM agencies, crypto regulatory/compliance consultants, FinTech digital transformation consultancies, Web3 IR firms, crypto-native management consulting boutiques.
- **Size:** 2–50 employees, $100k–$5M ARR. Prioritize **Established Advisory Practice** ($500k–$5M) for first 10 deals over Early-Stage ($100k–$500k) to validate economics.
- **Geography:** EU Continental only until 5-project gate. Tailor MiCA messaging for any P2 end-client brief.
- **Business model:** Retainer + project advisory ($5k–$30k/month). The **implementation contract** is what they currently lose to dev-first competitors.
- **India-based agencies excluded** — peer/competitor perception prevents sub-contractor trust (carried over from Trioangle Channel Partner pattern).

## Personas

### Persona 1 — Web3 Strategy Consultant
Founder / managing partner (28–45). Boutique Web3 advisory or tokenomics firm, 2–15 people. Deep crypto-native credibility (ETHGlobal, Mirror, crypto Twitter), **zero engineers**. Hands off to dev shops or scrambles for Solidity freelancers. Wants InnBlockchain as a silent build partner preserving their advisor identity. Budget authority: full; client builds $20k–$300k+.

### Persona 2 — FinTech Digital Transformation Consultant
Advises regulated FinTechs on blockchain strategy, MiCA compliance, tokenization roadmaps. Publishes on Finextra/PYMNTS/Banking Technology, speaks at Money20/20/FinTech Connect. Loses the build phase to vendors with compliance + engineering capability. Driven by **MiCA enforcement deadlines** — regulatory deadlines create non-negotiable urgency.

### Persona 3 — Crypto GTM & Tokenomics Advisor
Solo or small-team advisor on token launches, vesting schedules, community growth. **Primary goal is distinct from P1/P2: protecting personal reputation on a single client's token launch, not recurring revenue.** One smart contract failure in a vesting or sale contract ends their career. Reputational protection is the emotional driver.

## Qualifying Criteria

- **Strongest signal (P2 FinTech):** advisory firm confirms their FinTech client already has an **API-first backend live in production**. Skip preliminary discovery, move to technical scoping. Mirrors the FinTech Tier 0 qualifier.
- **Must-have:** actively advises clients who reach a technology build phase; comfortable sub-contracting; client build budget minimum **$20k+** for all verticals. Typical $20k–$300k per engagement.
- **P1/P3 budget type qualifier (crypto-native end-clients):** (1) founder-controlled wallet/multisig → 1–3 days; (2) DAO treasury vote → +2–4 weeks (offer a pre-written governance proposal template); (3) token-denominated → confirm stablecoin liquidity, not just vested tokens.
- **P2 procurement qualifier:** (1) CEO/CTO discretionary <$75k → 1–2 weeks; (2) CFO sign-off $75k+ → +4–6 weeks; (3) board/investor visibility → offer to co-present.
- **DQ:** pure content/PR agencies, pre-product-idea-stage-only firms, solo freelancers without advisory structure, ICO-scheme firms, firms with an exclusive dev partner already.

## Pain Points

1. **"We do the hard advisory work and lose the implementation contract"** (10/10) — financial + reputational. P3: career-ending.
2. **Unreliable freelance Solidity devs are a client relationship risk** (9/10) — P1/P3 Twitter/Braintrust sourcing; P2 equivalent: RFP vendors who build fast but ignore compliance.
3. **Smart contract security is outside our expertise** (9/10) — P1/P3 frame is on-chain exploit risk; P2 frame is compliance-blind builds failing regulatory audit.
4. **Can't scale advisory to include implementation without hiring engineers** (8/10) — P1/P2 firm scaling; P3 equivalent is reputational bandwidth.
5. **Scoping and pricing blockchain builds is impossible without a technical partner** (7/10).
6. **FinTech clients need blockchain integration but compliance and engineering are two separate problems** (8/10, P2-specific).

## Goals — Partner Dream Outcome

Become the go-to "strategy + build" firm in their niche — tokenomics to mainnet, compliance to deployment — with InnBlockchain as the invisible engineering arm.

**JTBD P1/P3:** *"When my blockchain advisory engagement reaches the implementation phase, I want a crypto-native delivery partner with proven smart contract depth and a structured sub-contracting model, so I can capture the full engagement lifecycle and never hand off implementation to a competitor again."*

**JTBD P2:** *"When my FinTech client's blockchain transformation reaches the build phase, I want an engineering partner who builds to my compliance specification and delivers audit-ready documentation as standard, so my client goes live without a regulatory incident and I remain the single accountable partner."*

## Situational Triggers

- Advisory firm just lost an implementation contract post-strategy
- Client closed a seed round and wants product in 4 weeks
- Solidity freelancer disappeared 3 weeks before token launch
- FinTech client needs MiCA-compliant infrastructure by regulator deadline
- **Exploit-reactive urgency (P1/P3):** reach out within 48 hours of major DeFi exploits. Monitor Rekt News and DeFi Llama feeds.
- **MiCA enforcement (P2):** monitor enforcement news, proactively reference in outreach.

## Budget Routing Rule

Sub-$20k crypto-native end-clients → route to [[InnBlockchain Crypto Products ICP]] (Trioangle clone scripts, $5k–$20k, live in 5–15 days). **P2 exception:** Crypto Products does not apply to FinTech clients (designed for crypto-native founders). Scope smaller integration or park relationship until budget grows.

**Re-engagement trigger:** coach the partner to check routed clients at 60/90 days. Bring them back to InnBlockchain custom dev when (1) user growth requires feature expansion, (2) funding round closes, (3) clone script limits hit (custom matching engine, proprietary compliance, licensing).

**Burned Builder routing signal:** *"My client tried a custom agency / Solidity freelancer and it failed — they lost $30k–$100k."* → Crypto Products Persona 5 (highest urgency). Close faster than first-timers. Framing: *"This team has deployed 800+ platforms — here are on-chain addresses to verify before you commit a dollar."*

## Non-Circumvention (NCNDA)

**Primary trust barrier for all three personas** — must be signed Day 1. The "800+ projects delivered" proof point can **backfire** as a circumvention fear; counter with: *"InnBlockchain has no direct sales motion for these markets; the partner model is permanent."*

## Non-Blockchain Client Briefs — Route to Trioangle

If a partner brings a non-blockchain brief (web/mobile app, marketplace, on-demand service, rental, mobility, e-learning), route to **[[Trioangle Channel Partner Personas]]** / Trioangle's Channel Partner ICP. Both InnBlockchain and Trioangle are group companies; the advisory firm's relationship is retained regardless of which entity delivers.

## Pre-Sales Tool

**Discovery Sprint** ($1,500–$4,000) — paid pre-sales scoping available for all partner types. Mirrors the Trioangle channel partner playbook.

## Objection Handling (Highlights)

- *"How do I know your smart contracts are production-grade?"* → audit-readiness as baseline, coordinate with CertiK/Hacken/Zellic, show completed audit report from comparable protocol before commit.
- *"How do we know you won't bypass us?"* → NCNDA Day 1, partner model is permanent.
- *"My client's budget is only $8k-$15k"* → Crypto Products routing (crypto-native only; not P2 FinTech).
