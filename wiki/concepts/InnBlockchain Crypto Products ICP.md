# InnBlockchain Crypto Products ICP

The **white-label clone script product line** covering crypto platforms: **CEX** (Binance/Coinbase-style), **P2P** (Paxful/Remitano-style), **DEX/DeFi** (Uniswap/Pancakeswap-style), **NFT marketplace** (OpenSea/Rarible-style). Price band **$5k–$20k**, functional platform launch in **5–15 business days** vs. 3–6 months custom build. Operated under the **[[Trioangle]]** brand (InnBlockchain's sister/group company). Designed for pre-raise / early-stage crypto founders who need to validate the product before committing to a full custom development budget.

## Who This Is For

Crypto-native entrepreneurs and DeFi ecosystem builders who understand blockchain fundamentals but lack the engineering depth to build exchange infrastructure, smart contracts, or NFT marketplaces from scratch. Also the sub-$20k routing target from [[InnBlockchain Channel Partner ICP]] and [[InnBlockchain Crypto Native ICP]].

**Crucial exclusion:** not appropriate for regulated FinTech clients (see P2 exception in Channel Partner ICP). Clone scripts are designed for crypto-native founders, not regulated FinTechs.

## Firmographics

- **Size:** 1–10 employees (founding team or solo)
- **Revenue:** Pre-revenue or early token/fee revenue
- **Stage:** Concept / pre-launch / token raise in progress
- **Geography priority:** **North America, UK, EU, Middle East** (high-intent, capital-rich, compliance-aware); secondary: SEA, Eastern Europe, LatAm, West Africa, diaspora communities in priority markets
- **Business model:** trading/withdrawal fees, LP incentives, NFT royalties, token listing fees

## Demographics & Psychographics

- **Age:** 22–40 | **Income:** $30k–$120k/yr or crypto portfolio equivalent
- **Location priority:** New York, Miami, London, Berlin, Amsterdam, Dubai, Abu Dhabi
- **Education:** CS/finance/econ; heavily self-educated via crypto Twitter, Bankless, YouTube
- **Identity:** *"Building the crypto infrastructure for my region"* or *"the Binance of [emerging market]"*
- **Risk tolerance:** high for market, moderate for vendor — demands security audits and code reviews

## Qualifying Criteria

- **Must-have:** $5k–$20k budget; defined blockchain target (Ethereum, BNB Chain, Polygon, etc.); clear revenue model (fees/royalties)
- **Nice-to-have:** initial liquidity partners or market-making plan; existing community/trading base; legal review started; VASP/crypto licence filed or in progress; MiCA-aligned, MAS-friendly, FCA-registered, or DIFC/ADGM/VARA-licensed jurisdiction
- **Hard DQ:** expects full custom DeFi for $500; wants source code only to resell; no basic blockchain understanding; purely speculative with no business plan; **operator targeting prohibited jurisdictions** (US state bans, China, Algeria, Egypt, Qatar, Morocco); **mixer / tumbler / privacy-coin-only use cases** (sanctions + brand risk); **anonymous operator refusing vendor KYC** (post-Tornado Cash enforcement); **ICO scaffolding with no underlying product**; **stolen-funds laundering pattern** (refuse and document)

## Price Band Anchors

Scope dial for the sales team in discovery:
- **$5k–$8k entry tier:** single-chain deployment, default branding, one KYC provider, one fiat rail or crypto-only
- **$9k–$13k standard tier:** 2–3 chains, custom branding, configurable KYC tiers, 2–3 fiat rails, admin tuning
- **$14k–$20k full tier:** 4+ chains, deep brand customisation, multi-provider KYC, full fiat rail set including mobile money (M-Pesa/bKash/GCash/Flutterwave), custom dispute/escrow rules, priority deployment

## Regulatory Framing by Priority Market

- **🇪🇺 EU — MiCA (in force):** CASP authorisation required; scripts ship MiCA-expected technical controls; licence itself is the buyer's legal process (refer to MiCA specialists). A German/Dutch/French buyer launching without naming MiCA is a red flag — educate or flag.
- **🇬🇧 UK — FCA cryptoasset regime (MLR 2017):** post-Brexit, distinct from MiCA. UK operators need FCA registration.
- **🇸🇬 Singapore — MAS Payment Services Act:** digital payment token licensing. SG buyers are typically sophisticated — confirm licensing strategy in call 1.
- **🇦🇪 UAE — three regulators:** DIFC (DFSA), ADGM (FSRA), VARA (Dubai mainland). Ask buyers *which* regulator. "UAE" without a specific answer = hasn't started licensing.
- **🇺🇸 US — FinCEN MSB + state-by-state MTL:** most expensive and longest path ($1M+ bonds, 12–18 months). Honest answer: $5k–$20k clone script + US compliance is not realistic for a solo founder in 2026 — refer out or redirect jurisdiction.

**Disqualifier escalation:** refuse sales to prohibited jurisdictions; document the refusal. One bad deal is not worth group-brand remediation cost.

## Pain Points

1. **Exchange infrastructure is prohibitively complex to build** (9/10) — custom would be 12–18 months and $200k–$500k
2. **Security anxiety — hacks, exploits, rug pulls** (9/10) — non-negotiable purchase blocker
3. **Regulatory and compliance uncertainty** (8/10) — VASP license? KYC/AML mandatory? Paralysis common
4. **Liquidity bootstrap problem** (7/10) — platform launches with no liquidity → no trades → dies
5. **Blockchain & wallet integration complexity** (6/10) — multi-chain feels like re-engineering

## Goals

- **Primary:** first trade / NFT sale processed within <15 days of purchase
- **Secondary:** $1k–$10k/mo in fees within 30–60 days; 500+ users, 50+ DAU within 60–90 days; third-party audit pre-launch

## Buyer Personas (Archetype-First)

Personas are defined by **who the buyer is emotionally**, not which product they intend to buy. Product match follows archetype identification.

| Archetype | Trioangle Product ICP equivalent | Primary Products |
|---|---|---|
| **Regional Market Pioneer** | Visionary Velocity Founder | CEX clone, P2P clone |
| **Diaspora Fintech Builder** | Diaspora Digital Entrepreneur | P2P clone (most common), CEX clone |
| **Web3 Protocol Architect** | *(unique — no direct equivalent)* | DEX / DeFi clones |
| **Community Platform Monetizer** | Small Business Digitalizer | NFT marketplace clone |
| **Burned Crypto Builder** | Burned Builder Comeback Founder | Any — vendor trust is the lens |

### Persona 1 — Regional Market Pioneer
CEO/co-founder, 25–38. Pre-launch. CEX clone in banking-enabled markets; P2P clone in unbanked/fiat-restricted markets. Has already decided to build — shopping for vendor, not deciding whether to launch. Budget: CEX $8k–$20k, P2P $5k–$12k. 3–5 week due diligence.

### Persona 2 — Diaspora Fintech Builder
Founder/investor, 28–42. Lives in US/UK/UAE/Canada, building for home country. P2P primarily (mobile money rails: M-Pesa, bKash, GCash), CEX for more developed home markets. Peer-driven research via diaspora groups. Higher budget tolerance $8k–$18k (stable diaspora income).

### Persona 3 — Web3 Protocol Architect
DEX/DeFi clones. Crypto-native, protocol mindset, technically literate.

### Persona 4 — Community Platform Monetizer
NFT marketplace buyer. Artist/creator community leader owning royalties and experience.

### Persona 5 — Burned Crypto Builder
Second-attempt buyer with deep vendor distrust after prior exploit or agency failure. **Highest-urgency archetype** — has already validated market opportunity, has capital, forensically evaluates vendors. Closes faster than first-timers once trust is established. Key intro framing: *"This team has deployed 800+ platforms — here are on-chain addresses to verify before you commit a dollar."*

## Situational Triggers

**Priority markets:** SEC spot Bitcoin ETF approval, MiCA regulatory clarity, Dubai/ADGM licensing windows, UK FCA tightening, pre-seed raise closed. **Secondary markets:** bull cycle onset, local competitor launch, country crypto legalization, Solidity freelancer abandonment, NFT community demanding marketplace, Paxful exit creating P2P vacuum, mobile money gap.

## Graduation Path — Clone Script → InnBlockchain Custom Dev

Every clone script deal is the **start** of a longer relationship, not a transaction. CRM, sales, and delivery treat every closed deal as a future [[InnBlockchain Crypto Native ICP]] lead.

### Re-Engagement Triggers (check 60 days and 90 days post-launch)

1. **User-growth feature pressure** — hitting clone limits (custom matching engine, proprietary compliance logic, bespoke token economics, new chain, non-standard fiat rail)
2. **Funding round closed** ($500k+ post-launch) — budget ceiling moved, custom dev on the table
3. **Regulatory escalation** — MiCA CASP, VARA tightening, new MAS guidance needing deeper customisation
4. **Exploit or near-miss** — immediate demand for deeper audit or hardened rebuild (handle carefully; lead with security audit motion)
5. **Acquisition conversation** — diligence surfaces code-ownership / audit questions

### Handoff Protocol

- Every Crypto Products deal CRM-tagged `graduation_candidate=true` with auto-created 60-day follow-up
- Check-in script (day 60/90): *"How's the platform running? What are you building next that the current setup doesn't cover?"* Listen, don't pitch.
- Internal handoff reassigns to InnBlockchain custom dev sales owner with full deal history; original rep stays in-loop on the first follow-up call
- **Partner-originated deals:** the advisor ([[InnBlockchain Channel Partner ICP]]) owns the re-introduction. NCNDA applies. No direct contact with end-client.
- Graduation deals are **not discounted** on the custom dev side — the clone script was production validation, not a down-payment

### Success Metric

Target **25% of clone script deals graduate to an InnBlockchain custom dev engagement within 12 months.** Baseline after first 20 deals.

## Competitive Math (Defensible)

Against custom dev agencies: **~96% cost saving** and **~97% timeline compression** at price-band midpoints ($12.5k clone vs. $350k custom; 10 business days vs. 15 months). ~20× cheaper, ~40× faster. Trade-off: production-tested template vs. blank repo — which is what most founders actually want.

## "800+ Platforms" Backfire Risk on Channel-Partner Deals

The 800+ proof point is the strongest trust builder with direct buyers and the **biggest circumvention-fear trigger** on deals originating through [[InnBlockchain Channel Partner ICP]]. On partner deals, reframe: *"InnBlockchain and Trioangle have no direct-sales motion into your client's segment for custom builds — partner model is permanent. The 800+ figure is clone script deployments, not the custom engagements we only take through partners."* Pair with NCNDA signed Day 1 before sharing any on-chain addresses or audit docs.

## Common Objections

- *"How secure is this? I can't risk user funds."* → KYC/AML, 2FA, anti-DDoS, cold storage, withdrawal whitelisting; 150+ countries since 2015; request security docs and on-chain transaction records before purchase
- *"Paxful already operates here. Why switch?"* → local mobile money rails, lower fees, accountability, faster onboarding
