# Trioangle Service Editorial Calendar

Publishing schedule for the Trioangle AI Engineering and Custom Development blog. Companion to [[Trioangle Content Strategy]] (strategy layer) and [[Trioangle Service ICP]] (buyer definitions). Covers 48 articles over 6 months at 2x/week cadence, with Month 7+ long-tail queue of 37 additional articles.

---

## Cadence and Format

**Schedule:** 2 posts/week, Monday and Thursday.
**Total 6-month output:** 48 articles (8/month).
**Cadence rationale:** Reduced from 3/week to protect quality on Pillar 7 AI engineering deep-dives (2,500-4,000 words, technical accuracy review required). A 2x cadence still delivers 52 articles in 6 months if maintained — sufficient for topical authority.
**Fallback trigger:** If writing throughput drops below 2 publish-ready articles/week for two consecutive weeks, drop to Mon-only (1x/week) temporarily and restore quality before resuming.

## Trigger-Based Outbound Distribution

**Rule:** All 48 articles publish to the blog on schedule for SEO indexing. Articles marked below are withheld from Channel 4 outbound sequences until a matching established-operator trigger event is detected.

**Triggers retired** (pre-traction Seed/Series A funding no longer valid per [[Trioangle Service ICP]]):
- Seed funding announcement
- Series A close

**Active triggers:**
| Article | Outbound Trigger |
|---------|-----------------|
| #77 (When to Hire Senior CTO/VP Eng) | CTO/VP Eng job posting at an established operator |
| #80 (Vertical Competitor Shipped AI) | Direct vertical competitor AI feature announcement (not a funding round) |
| #85-#92, #102-#108 (Pillar 7 vertical AI deep-dives) | Vertical competitor AI feature launch OR prospect posts ML/AI Engineer role |

**Channel 4 conditional gate (Month 2):** Activate Channel 4 outbound sequences only if trigger-detection tooling (LinkedIn Sales Navigator + Google Alerts/Mention + AppFollow/Sensor Tower + Crunchbase Pro) is operational with a named owner. If tooling is not live, defer to Month 3.

## Capacity and Quality Gates

**Pre-launch checklist** (confirm before Month 1 Week 1):
1. Named owner for each article type (Pillar 7 deep-dives require technical accuracy review)
2. Target lengths confirmed: 1,500-2,500 words for Awareness/Consideration; 2,500-4,000 for Pillar 7 technical deep-dives
3. Edit/review cycle defined: minimum one domain-knowledgeable pass before Pillar 7 articles publish
4. Month 4 quality checkpoint scheduled: review average time-on-page and Pillar 7 hub search rank velocity before committing Months 5-6 at full cadence

**Month 3 decision-stage warning:** Month 3 is 100% Pillar 7 — no decision-stage content. Decision-stage coverage through Month 3 is ~3 of 24 articles (~13%): #5 case study, #90 (adding AI without breaking stability), #91 (AI service isolation). If Month 2 pillar hub click-through is below target, pull #23 (Discovery Sprint) into Month 3.

## Distribution and Repurposing

Every article must feed at least one channel beyond the blog.

| Article Type | LinkedIn | Channel 4 Outbound | Newsletter |
|-------------|----------|-------------------|------------|
| Pillar 7 AI deep-dive | 3-5 key-insight post; tag 2-3 P5 ICP accounts | Via trigger-matched sequence only | P5-segment nurture if list exists |
| Pillar 2 trust/vendor | Opinion post ("3 things founders get wrong about IP ownership") | P.S. line in cold outreach when contextually relevant | All-segment nurture |
| Case study/post-mortem | Long-form LinkedIn story post | Lead follow-up #2 for matched vertical | Standalone email to full list |
| Channel-partner (🤝) | Do NOT post directly — for partner distribution only | N/A | Do NOT include in P5/P1/P2 nurture |

**LinkedIn cadence:** 2 posts/week, Mon + Thu, mirrors blog schedule. Pillar 7 articles post Monday (highest P5 LinkedIn activity mid-morning). Searchable articles: convert 3 core insights into a list post, link to blog at end. Shareable articles: standalone LinkedIn narrative, blog is the extended version.

**Newsletter (if email list exists):** Biweekly. Week 1-2: top Pillar 7 article + one AI insight. Week 3-4: Pillar 2 trust article or case study + Trioangle proof point. Every email CTA: AI Readiness Call (primary) or AI Audit Sprint page (warmer segments).

## Metrics Dashboard

### Weekly Targets (Track Every Friday)

| Metric | Target |
|--------|--------|
| Organic sessions — Pillar 7 hub | Growing MoM from Month 2 baseline |
| Free AI Readiness Call bookings | 2+/month by Month 2; 5+/month by Month 4 |
| Paid AI Audit Sprint bookings | 1+/quarter by Month 3 |
| LinkedIn engagement rate (Pillar 7 posts) | ≥3% per post (below 2% = reframe the hook) |
| Lead magnet downloads (AI Readiness PDF) | Track weekly from Month 1 launch |

### Monthly Review (Last Friday of Each Month)

- Top 3 articles by organic sessions: are they Pillar 7 or Pillar 2? If neither, diagnose.
- Pillar hub click-through rate: target >30% of hub visitors click at least one spoke article.
- AI Readiness Call booking source: double internal links to the article driving the most bookings.
- Decision-stage coverage: if below 8% of cumulative published articles, add one Decision-stage article to the next month.
- Cadence health: if 2+ slots missed in a month, investigate root cause and consider 1x/week fallback.
- Channel 4 trigger queue: trigger events detected this month? Reply rate on deployed articles?

### Quarterly Pivot Decision (End of Month 3 and Month 6)

Three go/no-go questions before confirming the next quarter:
1. Is Pillar 7 converting? Channel 4 reply rate below 5% after 3 months of trigger-matched deployment = content angle needs rework, not more volume.
2. Is custom-dev inbound growing? Pillar 1/2/3 organic sessions should increase from Month 4 onward. Flat by Month 6 = audit keyword targeting and internal linking.
3. Is the Pillar 7 expansion batch warranted? Only commission additional Pillar 7 articles if Channel 4 reply rates validate content ROI. Do not add volume on cadence alone.
