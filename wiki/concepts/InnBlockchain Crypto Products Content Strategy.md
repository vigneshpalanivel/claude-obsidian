# InnBlockchain Crypto Products Content Strategy

Content program for [[InnBlockchain Crypto Products ICP]] — crypto-native entrepreneurs buying Trioangle-branded clone scripts ($5k-$25k, 5-15 day delivery). 4 verticals × 5 pillars × 43 scored articles × 5 personas. The defining dynamic: **in crypto, claims mean nothing — verifiable on-chain proof is everything**.

## Pillars

1. **🚀 Exchange Launchpad & Tech Infrastructure** (TOFU→MOFU)
2. **🔐 Security & Smart Contract Safety** (TOFU→MOFU) — **#1 purchase blocker; universal trust foundation**
3. **⚖️ Compliance & Regulatory Navigator** (MOFU) — MiCA, FCA, DIFC/ADGM, SEC/FinCEN
4. **💧 Liquidity & Growth Playbook** (MOFU→Post)
5. **⭐ Proof & On-Chain Credibility** (BOFU) — case studies, on-chain addresses, competitor comparisons

## Priority Markets

- **Primary**: New York, Miami, London, Berlin, Amsterdam, Dubai, Abu Dhabi (US/UK/EU/ME)
- **Priority diaspora**: founders physically in NYC/London/Dubai/Toronto building for home markets — treat as priority
- **Secondary**: Singapore, Istanbul, Lagos, Kyiv, Buenos Aires

## AI Search as New TOFU

> [!key-insight] Priority market buyers query AI before Google
> ChatGPT, Perplexity, Gemini are now the first research step for US/UK/EU/ME buyers. Content must be citation-structured: direct answer in first 100 words, question-phrased H2 headers, FAQ schema, one citable claim per article. Track citations manually (fixed query harness, monthly) until third-party AI visibility tools mature.

## Format Mix & Tone

25% Technical Guides · 20% Security/Proof · 20% How-To Launchpad · 15% Comparison · 15% Case Studies · 5% Industry Reports.

**Tone rule**: never write "easy" or "simple" about smart contract security. Every claim needs verifiable proof. This ICP reads GitHub, uses Dune Analytics, and distrusts oversimplification.

## Competitor Benchmarks

Priority market buyers benchmark Trioangle against **B2Broker, AlphaPoint, Shift Markets, Hayvn, Rain** (MENA). Comparison content is a designated pillar (#5 Proof & On-Chain Credibility), not an afterthought. Silo: `/blog/trioangle-vs-*/` entries for each.
