# InnBlockchain Crypto Products Content Strategy

Content program for [[InnBlockchain Crypto Products ICP]] — crypto-native entrepreneurs buying Trioangle-branded clone scripts ($5k-$25k, 5-15 day delivery). 4 verticals × 5 pillars × 40 scored articles × 5 personas. The defining dynamic: **in crypto, claims mean nothing — verifiable on-chain proof is everything**.

## Verticals (1:1 with Product Lines)

| Vertical | Products | Primary Persona |
|----------|----------|-----------------|
| 🏦 CEX | Binance/Coinbase/Bybit clones | Regional Market Pioneer + Burned Builder |
| 🤝 P2P | Remitano/Paxful/LocalBitcoins clones | Diaspora Fintech Builder |
| 🔄 DEX/DeFi | Uniswap/PancakeSwap/GMX/dYdX/1inch clones | Web3 Protocol Architect |
| 🎨 NFT | OpenSea/Rarible clones, GameFi NFT | Community Platform Monetizer |

## Pillars

1. **🚀 Exchange Launchpad & Tech Infrastructure** (TOFU→MOFU)
2. **🔐 Security & Smart Contract Safety** (TOFU→MOFU) — **#1 purchase blocker; universal trust foundation**
3. **⚖️ Compliance & Regulatory Navigator** (MOFU) — MiCA, FCA, DIFC/ADGM, SEC/FinCEN
4. **💧 Liquidity & Growth Playbook** (MOFU→Post)
5. **⭐ Proof & On-Chain Credibility** (BOFU) — case studies, on-chain addresses, competitor comparisons

## Publishing Sequence

> [!key-insight] Security Hub first, then 4 vertical hubs simultaneously
> Article #1 (Are Crypto Clone Scripts Safe?) is the universal trust foundation and the link target for every subsequent article. Then CEX, P2P, DEX, NFT hubs publish in parallel Month 1. MOFU/BOFU layers on after vertical hubs exist to receive internal links.

First 12 posts locked: Security Hub → CEX hub → P2P hub → DEX hub → Security MOFU → CEX MOFU/BOFU → KYC/AML → NFT hub → CEX cost reframe → P2P case study → Fork vs White-label → Diaspora case study.

## Persona Distribution Rules

| Persona | Primary Channel | Secondary |
|---------|-----------------|-----------|
| Regional Market Pioneer | AI Search + Google + Telegram | LinkedIn (priority market) |
| **Diaspora Fintech Builder** | **Facebook diaspora groups + WhatsApp communities** (peer-driven; not search/Twitter) | LinkedIn, YouTube |
| **Web3 Protocol Architect** | GitHub, Crypto Twitter, Reddit r/defi/r/ethdev, Discord | Blog alone does NOT convert — sales must offer NDA code access + on-chain TVL proof |
| Community Platform Monetizer | YouTube, Discord, NFT communities | — |
| **Burned Crypto Builder** | #37 Restart Guide at TOFU; Reddit + Crypto Twitter | Longest cycle (4-8 weeks); NDA code access early |

## Priority Markets

- **Primary**: New York, Miami, London, Berlin, Amsterdam, Dubai, Abu Dhabi (US/UK/EU/ME)
- **Priority diaspora**: founders physically in NYC/London/Dubai/Toronto building for home markets — treat as priority
- **Secondary**: Singapore, Istanbul, Lagos, Kyiv, Buenos Aires

## AI Search as New TOFU

> [!key-insight] Priority market buyers query AI before Google
> ChatGPT, Perplexity, Gemini are now the first research step for US/UK/EU/ME buyers. Content must be citation-structured: clear headings, direct answers, structured data, authoritative citations. Track citations manually (fixed query harness, monthly) until third-party AI visibility tools mature.

## Format Mix & Tone

25% Technical Guides · 20% Security/Proof · 20% How-To Launchpad · 15% Comparison · 15% Case Studies · 5% Industry Reports.

**Tone rule**: never write "easy" or "simple" about smart contract security. Every claim needs verifiable proof. This ICP reads GitHub, uses Dune Analytics, and distrusts oversimplification.

## Competitor Benchmarks

Priority market buyers benchmark Trioangle against **B2Broker, AlphaPoint, Shift Markets, Hayvn, Rain** (MENA). Comparison content is a designated pillar (#5 Proof & On-Chain Credibility), not an afterthought. Silo: `/blog/trioangle-vs-*/` entries for each.
