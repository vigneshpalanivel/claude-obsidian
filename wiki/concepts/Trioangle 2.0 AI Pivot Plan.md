# Trioangle 2.0 AI Pivot Plan

Strategic plan to reposition [[Trioangle]] from a marketplace clone script + custom dev shop into an **AI-powered services + reseller company targeting SMBs**, without losing existing revenue during the transition. 18-month horizon in four phases.

**Target segment:** SMBs (not enterprise). No SaaS product line.
**Geographic scope:** India-first + English-speaking global SMB (US/UK/SEA/MENA).
**Revenue mechanism:** productized services + monthly retainers + **agency reseller channel** + training business.

---

## Strategic Thesis

**Three parallel goals:**

1. **Stabilize cash flow** using existing clients + quick AI add-ons (survive)
2. **Build AI engineering capability** without losing billable hours (capacity)
3. **Reposition brand + sales engine** toward SMB AI services with compounding revenue mechanisms (growth)

### Why SMB (and why now)

- 800+ existing Trioangle clone script customers are overwhelmingly SMB — built-in upsell base
- India-based delivery cost hits the right price point for global SMB
- SMBs buy productized packages in 1–4 week cycles (not RFPs)
- Monthly retainers compound; enterprise project cycles don't
- Avoids direct competition with Accenture, Deloitte, TCS AI practices

### Opportunity Cost of NOT Pivoting

If Trioangle does nothing, the base case is:
- Clone script revenue declines 20-35% over 18 months as vibe coding matures (per [[Research: Running a Software Dev Company Post-Vibe-Coding]])
- Custom dev margins compress 15-25% as AI tooling commoditizes the work
- Junior hiring becomes net-negative ROI
- Competitors with AI-native teams absorb the high-margin segments

The pivot isn't optional. It's a question of **whether the pivot is ahead of or behind the revenue decline curve.**

### Positioning Rule (from [[Trioangle Service ICP]])

> Never position on "we build faster with AI." Position on **productized packages, fixed pricing, and delivery certainty.** Trioangle's 800+ SMB-scale deployments since 2015 is the real moat.

## Target Customer — 5 SMB AI Personas + 1 Channel

### Direct Buyers (Personas A, C, D, E)

**Persona A — Solo Platform Founder**
- Budget: ₹40K–₹3L; decision time 1–2 weeks
- Existing Trioangle clone script operator; no dev team
- Wants: productized AI features (chatbot, search, moderation)
- Channel: WhatsApp, existing customer base, Clutch

**Persona C — Small Business Automator**
- Budget: ₹50K–₹3L; decision time 1–3 weeks
- Real estate broker, clinic, coaching biz, ecommerce (<50 employees)
- Wants: WhatsApp AI, internal doc Q&A, lead qualification, ops automation
- Channel: LinkedIn, local chambers, WhatsApp, referrals

**Persona D — Mid-Market SaaS Founder**
- Budget: ₹3L–₹15L; decision time 3–8 weeks
- Runs SaaS at ₹50L–₹5Cr ARR; 1-2 devs, no AI expertise
- Wants: AI features to compete (smart search, assistant, automation)
- Channel: LinkedIn ABM, Product Hunt, SaaS communities

**Persona E — Content/Creator Economy Operator**
- Budget: ₹50K–₹4L; decision time 2–6 weeks
- Marketplace, D2C brand, creator platform
- Wants: AI moderation, personalization, chatbots
- Channel: Instagram/X, creator communities, webinars

### Reseller Partner (Persona B — treated separately)

**Persona B — Small Agency / Studio Owner (Reseller)**
- Agency size: 5–15 people serving local SMB clients
- They do not buy AI for themselves — they **resell** white-labeled AI packages to their clients
- Trioangle delivers; agency brands + keeps 20-40% margin
- Channel: LinkedIn, agency communities, partner outreach
- Single Persona B reseller can generate 3-10 end-customer deals

**Why separate this:** Persona B is a channel multiplier, not a customer. One agency relationship is worth 3-10 direct customers. The Agency Reseller Program (see Phase 1) is the **primary compounding mechanism replacing SaaS.**

## Compounding Revenue Mechanisms (Replacing SaaS)

Since there is no SaaS product, Trioangle must build **three compounding mechanisms** that aren't linear with headcount:

### 1. Agency Reseller Program (Primary)
White-label AI packages sold through 15-25 agency partners by M18. Each agency drives 3-10 end-customer deals/year with minimal Trioangle sales effort.

### 2. Training Business
AI implementation workshops for SMB founders at ₹25K-₹1L per session. Group workshops are 1-to-many revenue.

### 3. Community Retainer Tier
Shared AI support queue for smaller SMBs at ₹5K-₹8K/month. 100+ customers on shared support = ₹5-8L/month high-margin recurring.

These mechanisms together should produce **25-35% of revenue by M18** — enough margin to avoid the pure services trap.

## The Four Phases

```
Phase 0 — Foundation       (Months 1-3)   Stabilize cash, pick champions, quick wins
Phase 1 — Capability       (Months 4-6)   Build AI delivery pod; productize; launch Reseller Program
Phase 2 — Specialization   (Months 7-10)  Vertical lock; rebrand; sales engine
Phase 3 — Compound         (Months 11-14) Retainers + resellers + community tier
Phase 4 — Market Position  (Months 15-18) Vertical authority; training business; IP
```

Phases run in **parallel tracks** — legacy revenue, AI capability, and brand work happen simultaneously.

## Phase 0 — Foundation (Months 1-3)

**Objective:** Don't break cash flow. Plant AI seeds via existing customer base.

### Financial Triage (Week 1)
- Calculate runway in months
- List top 10 revenue clients → stable vs. at-risk
- Identify the 20% of services generating 80% of margin
- **Baseline CAC calculation** — what does it cost now to acquire a clone script customer? This is your starting benchmark.

### Team Assessment (Week 1-2)
- Identify 2-3 AI champions — curious, strong Python/JS, willing to learn
- Other devs continue current engagements (protect revenue)
- Do not announce pivot publicly yet
- **Note:** training timeline to full independence is **4-6 months realistic** (not 2-3). Plan for senior engineer hours to be pulled into supervision during this period.

### Quick Revenue Wins — Existing Customer Outreach (Weeks 2-8)

**Systematic outreach to 800+ existing Trioangle customers.** Assuming 3% conversion and ₹1L avg ticket: **₹24L pipeline from existing base alone.**

Process:
1. Segment 800 customers by vertical + activity level
2. Prioritize 150 most-active operators (Personas A, C mostly)
3. Personalized WhatsApp/email outreach — "we can add AI X to your platform"
4. Aim: 30 Scoping Calls in first 6 weeks → 10-15 closed deals

**Target:** ₹15L–₹25L new AI add-on revenue signed inside 30-45 days.

### Legacy Clone Script Business — Reframe, Don't Kill

Clone script sales continue as **lead-generation engine for AI upsells.** Every new clone script customer is a 6-month future AI buyer.

- Keep marketing + delivery for clone scripts unchanged
- Add "AI-ready" badge to clone script offerings (chatbot-ready, WhatsApp-ready)
- Include 1-month free AI Scoping Call with every clone script sale

Target: legacy clone script revenue stays **above 50% of baseline through M14**, declining thereafter — not zeroed out by M18.

### Capability Seeds (Weeks 2-12)

Champions follow [[AI Engineering Learning Roadmap]] starting at Phase 3 (they have SWE foundation):
- Week 1-2: LLM APIs + prompt engineering
- Week 3-4: First RAG chatbot (LangChain + pgvector)
- Week 5-6: n8n + WhatsApp AI
- Week 7-8: First paid AI project (supervised)
- Week 9-12: Ship 2-3 more; document patterns as **reusable templates** (critical for productization later)

### Credibility Setup (Week 1-2)

Apply for **Anthropic Startup Program** and **OpenAI Startup Program** immediately. These are free applications and provide:
- API credits (lowers delivery cost during training period)
- "Built on Claude / Built on OpenAI" logo — a credibility signal to SMB clients choosing between Trioangle and a generic local dev shop
- All major IT firms (TCS, Infosys, HCLTech) use OpenAI partnership logos as trust signals; same principle applies at SMB scale

This costs nothing and takes 1 hour. Do it in Week 1.

- [ ] 2-3 engineers AI-capable (with supervision)
- [ ] 3-5 AI projects in flight or shipped

**Objective:** Build AI delivery pod; productize services; **launch Agency Reseller Program pilot.**

### Team Restructure — Three Pods

| Pod | Share | Purpose |
|-----|-------|---------|
| Legacy Pod | 50-60% | Existing mobile/web/clone work; also AI-ready upselling |
| AI Integration Pod | 30-40% | Productized services delivery |

### Head of Delivery Hire (Month 6 Target)

**Critical operational hire.** Founder cannot personally own cash management + hiring + delivery leadership + sales + brand. By end of Phase 1, a Head of Delivery handles:
- Day-to-day project delivery + quality
- Team resourcing across pods
- Retainer renewals
- Client escalations

Frees founder for sales, strategy, reseller program, brand. Without this hire, execution bottlenecks on founder bandwidth.

### Productized AI Services (Fixed Price)

3 services live with **Stripe/Razorpay checkout** for packages under ₹1L:

1. **AI Customer Support Bot** — ₹75K–₹2L + ₹10-30K/mo retainer, 2 weeks
2. **RAG Knowledge Assistant** — ₹1.5L–₹4L + ₹20-50K/mo retainer, 2-3 weeks
3. **WhatsApp AI Automation** — ₹40K–₹1.2L + ₹10-25K/mo retainer, 1-2 weeks

**Every project ships with observability day 1** — Langfuse or LangSmith. Quality is the #1 production barrier (33% of orgs, per [[LangChain-State-of-Agent-Engineering-2026]]).

### Agency Reseller Program — Pilot (Months 5-6)

**Recruit 5 pilot agency partners (Persona B).** Structure:

- Trioangle delivers + supports; agency brands + sells
- 30-40% gross margin to agency on one-time package
- 20% margin on retainer (ongoing relationship with agency)
- Dedicated WhatsApp channel for reseller support
- Co-branded case study after 3 deals

Target end of Phase 1: 5 agency pilots, 3-8 end-customer deals through resellers (proof point).

### Sales Motion + CAC Tracking

- **WhatsApp-first** for Personas A, C — live response times
- **LinkedIn ABM** for Personas B, D, E — trigger-based
- **Existing client upsells** — continue systematic outreach
- **Track CAC + LTV/CAC weekly** — target CAC payback <6 months

**Do not add:** fine-tuning, on-prem, dedicated teams. Not SMB.

### Trioangle AI Delivery Kit — Internal Platform (Phase 2 Target)

By Month 8-9 (after 10-15 projects delivered), name and document the reusable internal toolkit as the **"Trioangle AI Delivery Kit"**. This is not a public product — it is an internal asset:

- Pre-configured RAG pipeline (LangChain + pgvector + Langfuse) ready to deploy
- Agent templates for support bot, WhatsApp automation, lead qualifier
- Observability stack pre-wired (evals, cost tracking, alert rules)
- Razorpay/WhatsApp/Shopify integration connectors
- Deployment scripts + client handover checklist

**Why this matters:**
1. Every new project starts from the kit, not from scratch → delivery time drops from 2 weeks to 3-5 days for repeat package types
2. Revenue per engineer rises without adding headcount
3. It becomes what you sell to reseller agencies: "you get our platform, not just our people"
4. It's the honest version of what TCS calls "AI WisdomNext" and Infosys calls "Topaz Fabric" — the same open-source stack, wrapped in your delivery process and domain knowledge

**This is how the rev/head target of ₹18-25L+ actually becomes achievable.** Without it, you are billing hours. With it, you are billing IP.

### Retainer Management — Retention Discipline

At 100+ retainer customers, retention is everything. Targets:

- **Monthly churn <5%** (industry SMB SaaS benchmark)
- **Net revenue retention >100%** (expansion > churn)
- **Dedicated renewal ops** — Head of Delivery owns this
- **Quarterly value reports** — auto-generated show of work (response times saved, issues caught, costs reduced)

Even at 5% monthly churn, 100 customers = 5 lost/month → plan sales capacity to replace + grow.
