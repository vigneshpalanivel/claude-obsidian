## 🚫 Hard rules (memorise — these never relax)

- **Never publish article without CORE-EEAT AI scoring** (Phase 3 step 17 — runs AFTER Vignesh compliance corrections applied, BEFORE final batch approval. Scoring an uncorrected draft wastes the call AND can produce a false high score on regime-leaked content).
- **Hard stops before Phase 3** (Pre-publish): LP returns 404 · regime framing uncertain on MiCA boundary · Pain Point 5 cluster article + W2 validation not passed → do NOT proceed; escalate.
- **Never derive article classification from content** — always look up from Editorial Calendar (Plan rev 9 Lookup Protocol).
- **Persona definitions live in ICP docs ONLY** — third-party templates use overlapping names with different meanings (Tom ≠ compliance officer in any third-party template; in our ICP Tom = FinTech CTO).
- **Apply per-track voice modifier** per article tag — Felix asset-first · Sam ROI-first · Tom technical-first · Carol compliance-first.
- **Cross-pillar articles use `[Both]` split CTA footer** — never blend voices in the same paragraph.
- **All regulatory references date-stamped** (e.g., "MiFID II Art. 4(1)(15) — as of 2026") per AEO Principle 8.
- **Em-dashes for parenthetical asides, US spelling, numerals 10+, no marketing voice** (per CW Content Style Guide formatting standards).

## ☎️ Backup / escalation

- Regulatory uncertainty → **Vignesh** (same day; never make regulatory judgment call yourself)
- Article slot dependency on LP not shipped → **Dev** (build status) + **Vignesh** (slot decision)
- Vignesh review SLA slipping on Tue accuracy / Thu compliance → escalate at Weekly Fri review · switch from 100% to 25% spot-check per WS13 delegated-work table
- Compliance review SLA slipping → **Vignesh + monthly review** tracks
- Style Guide ambiguity → **Vignesh decision** (per WS7 § CW Content Style Guide)
- CORE-EEAT score below threshold → iterate per Phase 2 step → re-submit before Phase 3 sign-off is final (don't ship below threshold)
