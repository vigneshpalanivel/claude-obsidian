## Global Writer Briefs — Apply to All Templates

### AI Search Structuring Requirements

Priority market buyers (US/UK/EU/Middle East) query ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Gemini **before** Google. Every article must be structured to earn AI citations — this is the primary TOFU channel for P1, P3, and P5.

| Requirement | Implementation |
|---|---|
| **Direct answer in first 100 words** | Answer the article's core question completely in paragraph 1. AI models cite the first citable sentence, not the conclusion. |
| **Question-phrased H2 headers** | At least 3 H2 headers phrased as exact queries (e.g., "What is a Binance clone script?", "How long does it take to launch a crypto exchange?"). |
| **One citable claim per article** | Include one specific, verifiable stat or figure with a source (on-chain data, audit cost range, deployment count). |
| **FAQ schema** | Implement schema.org/FAQPage structured data on every Q&A section. |
| **Authoritative external citations** | Link to at least one external authority: audit firm (Certik, Hacken, Trail of Bits), regulatory body (FCA, MiCA), on-chain data source (Dune Analytics, DeFi Llama). |

### Priority Market vs Secondary Market Variants

| Element | Priority Market (US/UK/EU/ME) | Secondary Market (SE Asia, Africa, LatAm, Eastern Europe) |
|---|---|---|
| **Regulatory context** | Name the specific framework: MiCA (EU), FCA (UK), DIFC/ADGM or VARA (UAE), SEC/FinCEN (US with redirect note) | VASP licensing, local frameworks; less compliance depth needed |
| **Competitor comparisons** | B2Broker, AlphaPoint, Shift Markets (US/EU); Hayvn, Rain (MENA) | Less relevant — price and speed are the primary objections |
| **CTA** | Demo booking form | WhatsApp intro preferred — "Chat with our team on WhatsApp → See the platform live" |
| **Trust signals** | On-chain proof + third-party audit report + compliance partner list | Regional founder case studies in the buyer's exact market + same-timezone support |
| **Distribution** | AI Search + Google SEO + Crypto Twitter/X + Telegram | Telegram crypto groups + WhatsApp communities (P2P/P1); local crypto meetup seeding |

> [!WARNING] P2 (Diaspora Fintech Builder) Distribution Exception
> Persona 2 is **peer-driven** — decisions come from diaspora community referrals, not search or Crypto Twitter. Any article targeting P2 must be actively seeded in **diaspora Facebook groups and WhatsApp communities** as part of its distribution plan. SEO alone will not surface content to this persona.

### Security Claim Compliance Check

Before submitting any draft, writers must verify:
1. Every security claim links to a checkable proof source (on-chain address, audit report URL, or note that it is available under NDA)
2. The words "easy" or "simple" do not appear in any context describing smart contract security or audit processes — P3 and P5 will distrust this framing
3. "Audited" is never used without specifying the auditing firm and linking to documentation
4. Every BOFU CTA routes to **verifiable proof** (on-chain deployment, live demo with admin panel access, audit docs) — not a feature list

### P3 (Web3 Protocol Architect) Content-to-Sales Handoff

Blog content alone **will not convert P3**. The article's job is to earn enough trust to get P3 onto a sales call. The BOFU CTA for all DEX/DeFi articles must offer:
- NDA-protected code access pre-sale
- On-chain contract addresses of live DEX deployments (verifiable on Etherscan/BSCScan)
- Third-party audit report download or request

Writers must include this handoff note in every DEX/DeFi BOFU article: *"Before committing, request NDA-protected code access and our audit documentation — [CTA link]."* This is not optional — it is the conversion mechanism for P3.
