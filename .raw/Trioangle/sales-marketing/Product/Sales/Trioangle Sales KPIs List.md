> [!WARNING] Scope — Product ICP only · Do not use for Service ICP
> These KPIs were defined for the **Product ICP** sales motion (clone scripts — high-volume, low-ACV, short cycles). They do **NOT** apply to the Service ICP, where deal sizes are $10k–$120k+, cycles run 45–180 days, sprint conversion gates the funnel, and per-deal economics — not call-volume ratios — drive forecasting.
>
> A Service-ICP-specific KPI set is pending. Until it exists, do not cite, copy, or import metrics from this document into any Service ICP material — including [[Trioangle - Sales Playbook - Service]] §15 (which has its own Service-calibrated KPI ranges).
>
> Some metric *names* below also appear in Service-ICP KPI thinking (e.g., demo call ratio, leads-to-opportunities ratio). The names overlap; the **target ranges and how they're interpreted do not**. Don't blend the two.
>
> **Last marked Product-ICP-only:** 2026-05-09 by Vignesh.

# Sales KPIs List for OKR — Product ICP

- No response rate
- Leads to Opportunities ratio
- Opportunities to Sales ratio
- Calls ratio / Demo call ratio
- Average sale value
- Increasing live chat count / online hours
- Increasing USP count
- Receiving the product updates
- Increasing customers list for call
- Reduce missed chat counts
- Live chat engagement rate
- Lead nurturing cycle time
- Email open rate and click through rate
- Pre-sale quote conversion ratio
