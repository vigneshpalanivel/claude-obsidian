# The Problem

---

## 01 · The Double Problem

### Market Shift
- AI-augmented dev (Cursor, Lovable, Bolt) is plateauing
- *"We build software faster"* is now commodity
- Next wave: **AI services delivered into SMB businesses**

### Transactional ICP
- 800+ projects since 2015 → only **~30 still operating**
- **96% mortality** — structural to pre-revenue startup buyers
- Every month resets to zero · no compounding base

---

## 02 · The Strategic Response

### Move 1 — Pivot to AI Services
Stop selling "faster software." Sell **AI features built into live platforms.**
8 verticals · 800+ deployments = the moat AI agencies can't match.

### Move 2 — Fix the ICP
Recurring is a **buyer problem, not a pricing problem.**
Existing operators (12+ months live, revenue traction) have to keep building forever.

**One move fixes both:**
- Recurring revenue → through cadence
- Higher deal size → 3–10× ASP vs clone scripts

# The Strategy

---

## 03 · Two-Track Strategy

### Track 1 — Product ICP · *Cash Engine*
- Clone scripts continue per existing playbook
- AI add-on pitch at handover (opportunistic only)
- **Not the recurring engine** — accept the transactional shape

### Track 2 — AI Services for Existing Operators · *Growth Engine*
- Position: **AI specialist for SMB platform operators**
- Target: Service ICP **P5 direct** · P3 & P4 channel only
- Filter: 12+ months operational, revenue traction
- **Two-axis advantage:** higher ASP *and* recurring cadence
- *Phase 0 outbound active now*

---

## 04 · Why AI-Specialist Positioning Wins

- **General software = commodity in 2026** — every dev shop offers it
- **AI engineering still requires real domain expertise**
- **Trioangle's moat:** 800+ platforms across 8 verticals
- **Operators face board pressure to ship AI now** — not "should we?" but "who can?"
- **AI lands the relationship · full-service expands it** — one account, one vendor

---

## 05 · Target Personas

| Persona | Profile | Access |
|---|---|---|
| **P5** · AI-Forward Platform Operator | CTO/CPO running a live platform — needs AI, no in-house ML | **Direct** |
| **P4** · MENA Relationship | UAE/KSA family-group / Gulf-funded operating business | Channel only |
| **P3** · Digital Transformation Enterprise | Head of Digital at offline business being disrupted | Channel only |

**Deprioritise P1 (Scaling CTO) and P2 (Founder-CTO)** — startup-shaped, recreate the mortality risk.

---

## 06 · Why Existing Operators Generate Recurring Demand

1. **Production reality** — edge cases, payment changes, compliance shifts every quarter
2. **Competitive pressure** — competitors force the roadmap, not internal choice
3. **Successful operators expand** — new verticals, regions, payment methods
4. **Operations accumulate friction** — support backlogs become platform tweaks
5. **AI is a roadmap, not a feature** — Audit Sprint output *is* the project queue

**All four ICP conditions must hold:** revenue traction · competitive pressure · weak in-house · growth ambition.

---

## 07 · Land with AI, Expand with Full-Service

### Land — first project is always AI
*Dynamic pricing · intelligent dispatch · RAG · voice agent · AI moderation*

### Impress — fixed scope, fixed price, fixed date
A/B-tested business-metric lift · day-1 observability

### Expand — full-service follows naturally
Web · mobile · new verticals · payment gateways · platform scaling

**One account contact. One delivery brand. No vendor handoff.**
